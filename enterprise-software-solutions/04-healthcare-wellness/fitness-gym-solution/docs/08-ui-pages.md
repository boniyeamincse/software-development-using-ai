# 08 - Fitness & Gym UI Pages

## 1. Member App Home (The "Daily Pulse")
- Primary Action: "Digital Entry QR Code."
- "My Next Class" card with "Add to Calendar" button.
- "Workout of the Day" visual guide with video previews.

## 2. Interactive Class Scheduler
- Mobile-optimized grid view of group sessions with "Spots Left" indicators.
- Filter system: "By Trainer," "By Intensity," "By Time."

## 3. Gym Staff Command Center
- Live "Occupancy Gauge" showing how many members are currently in the facility.
- Check-in ticker with member photos for physical identity verification.
- "Maintenance Task List" for equipment repairs.

## 4. Personal Trainer Workspace
- Client roster view showing "Last Workout Completed" and "Missed Sessions."
- Drag-and-drop routine builder with library of 500+ exercises.
- Message hub for 1-on-1 client coaching.

## 5. Pro-Shop & Inventory Console
- Visual inventory grid for supplements and gym gear.
- POS modal for quick check-out at the front desk.

## 6. Executive Operations Panel
- Key Charts: "Peak Usage Times," "Annual Churn Rate," "Revenue per Sq Foot."
- Marketing dashboard for tracking "Trial Pass" conversion rates.
