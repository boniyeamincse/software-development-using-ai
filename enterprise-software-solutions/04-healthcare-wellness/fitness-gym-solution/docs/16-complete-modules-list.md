# 16 - Fitness & Gym Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Fitness & Progression Hub
- **Universal Workout Master**: customized routines and deep exercise libraries.
- **Precision Progress Hub**: tracking weights, reps, sets, and personal bests.
- **Biometric integrity Hub**: managing sensitive user physical measurements safely.
- **Wearable Data Sync Bridge**: integrating with global ecosystem (Apple Health, etc.).

## 2. engagement & social Suite
- **Dynamic Challenge Master**: managing group and individual fitness competitions.
- **Leaderboard & Community Hub**: real-time results for engagement and motivation.
- **Rewards & gamification Hub**: managing points, tiers, and badges for progress.
- **AI nudge & Habit orchestrator**: automated reminders for activity and hydration.

## 3. Training & coaching Workspace
- **Secure coach messaging**: encrypted channel for personal training interaction.
- **Program & Goal Hub**: collaborative tracking between coaches and members.
- **professional coaching Hub**: specialized dashboard for personal trainers.
- **Training Appointment Master**: managing schedules and session credits.

## 4. nutrition & Wellness Engine
- **Strategic Macro Master**: (Optional) managing meal logs and nutrient tracking.
- **AI Workout & meal Recommender**: personalized suggestions based on goals.
- **Fitness Assessment Master**: digital tools for baseline and follow-up testing.
- **Wellness Content Library**: (Optional) managing fitness videos and articles.

## 5. Operations & Membership
- **Subscription & Billing Hub**: managing tiers, trials, and automated renewals.
- **Facility Access & Entry Bridge**: (Optional) integration with gym turnstiles.
- **Class & Event Scheduler**: managing group fitness and workshop booking.
- **Executive Revenue Dashboard**: high-level visibility into enterprise health.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Biometric Privacy Hub**: high-security controls for physical and health data.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular coach versus admin permissions.
- **Immutable Membership Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant logging for all transactions.
- **Global Settings & Locales**: managing gym-specific rules and calendars.
