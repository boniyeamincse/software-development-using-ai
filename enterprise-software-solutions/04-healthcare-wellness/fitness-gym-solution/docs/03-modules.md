# 03 - Fitness & Gym System Modules

## Membership & Subscription Engine
Manages the logic for contracts, billing cycles, and member status transitions.

## Facility Access & Hardware Bridge
The integration layer for turnstiles, RFID readers, and IoT door controllers.

## Class & PT Scheduler
The core orchestration module for group sessions and personal training appointments.

## Member Engagement App
The mobile-first portal for workouts, bookings, and community features.

## Staff Operations Dashboard
The primary administrative interface for check-ins, sales, and facility management.

## Inventory & Retail POS
Manages the gym's pro-shop and physical merchandise stock.

## Financial & Growth Analytics
The reporting suite for revenue trends, churn analysis, and instructor performance.
