# 02 - Fitness & Gym Core Features

## 1. Digital Membership & Billing
- **Subscription Management**: Automated monthly recurring billing for tiered plans (Standard, Premium, Platinum).
- **Self-Service Freeze/Cancel**: Members can manage their own status according to gym policy.
- **Multi-Location Access**: "Passport" features allowing members to visit any branch in the network.

## 2. Integrated Access Control
- **QR/NFC Mobile Entry**: Members use the gym app to unlock turnstiles or doors.
- **RFID Tag Sync**: Pairing physical tags with member profiles for instant identification.
- **Blacklist Logic**: Immediate access denial for suspended or unpaid memberships.

## 3. Class & Personal Training Hub
- **Interactive Schedule**: Real-time booking for Yoga, HIIT, Crossfit, and other group classes.
- **Smart Waitlists**: Automated notifications to the next member in line when a spot opens.
- **PT Matching**: Booking platform for 1-on-1 personal training sessions based on trainer availability.

## 4. Mobile Workout & Progress Tracker
- **Integrated Workout Library**: Hundreds of pre-built exercises with video guidance.
- **Custom Plan Creation**: Trainers can push personalized routines directly to their clients' apps.
- **Progress Visualizer**: In-app tracking for weight, body fat %, and strength milestones.

## 5. Staff & Instructor Tools
- **Roster Management**: Scheduling instructors and trainers across multiple zones/branches.
- **Check-in Analytics**: Real-time view of gym capacity and peak-hour heatmaps.
- **Lead Management**: Integrated CRM for sales staff to track prospective members and trial passes.

## 6. Pro-Shop & POS Integration
- **In-App Supplements Store**: One-click ordering for protein, gear, and drinks.
- **Inventory Sync**: Tracking stock levels for equipment and retail items at the front desk.
- **Contactless Payments**: Secure processing via Apple Pay, Google Pay, and saved member cards.
