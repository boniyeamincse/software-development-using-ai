# 13 - Fitness & Gym Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Vision Personal Trainer**: Bedside/Gym mirror assistance that corrects squat or deadlift form in real-time.
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Sensors on treadmills and weight machines that predict cable/motor failure before it happens.
- **AI-Driven Nutrition Sync**: Automatically suggesting post-workout supplements based on the specific routine completed.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Connected Equipment Integration**: Syncing directly with Peloton, Technogym, and Life Fitness cloud accounts.
- **Global Gym Network**: Universal "Roaming" agreements between independent gyms using the software ecosystem.

## Modern Experiences
- **Metaverse Workout Partners**: Members at different branches working out together in a shared AR/VR space.
- **Biometric Health Kiosks**: 360-degree body scanning integrated with the member app for visual progress tracking.
- **Voice-Activated Gym**: "Siri, check me into the Yoga class and open the locker room door."
