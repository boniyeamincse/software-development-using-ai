# 16 - Patient Feedback Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Interaction & Capture Hub
- **Universal Intake Master**: central hub for SMS, email, app, and QR-based surveys.
- **Survey Logic orchestrator**: managing schedules, frequencies, and branching logic.
- **real-time Metrics Generator**: instant calculation of NPS, CSAT, and CES scores.
- **Detractor Alert Gateway**: automated notifications for high-priority negative scores.

## 2. Analysis & Insights Suite
- **LLM Sentiment Master**: AI-driven analysis of comments for tone and intent.
- **Thematic clustering Engine**: automatically grouping feedback into clinical topics.
- **Sentiment Trend Tracker**: monitoring shifts in patient perception over time.
- **Provider-specific Insight Sidebar**: (Optional) providing feedback context to clinicians.

## 3. service Recovery & Workflow
- **Recovery Life-cycle Master**: structured state-machine for managing issues.
- **Remediation Task Hub**: assigned, tracked, and verified actions for feedback.
- **Internal Collaboration Workspace**: area for managers to discuss and resolve cases.
- **Closed-Loop Notification Bridge**: informing the patient once an issue is resolved.

## 4. performance & Reporting
- **Executive experience Dashboard**: high-level visibility into enterprise-wide sentiment.
- **Provider & Team Benchmark Hub**: comparing metrics against internal and external goals.
- **Clinic Health analytics**: identifying top-performing and at-risk locations.
- **Custom Report Architect**: powerful tools for creating stakeholder-specific data views.

## 5. Trust, Privacy & Data Sync
- **HIPAA Masking & Anonymity Hub**: high-security controls for patient identity.
- **Immutable Feedback WORM Vault**: tamper-proof logging of every submission.
- **Aggregated Health Bridge (HIE)**: syncing anonymized metrics with regional servers.
- **EHR 360-degree Context Hub**: linking experience data with clinical profiles.

## 6. Intelligence & Administration
- **Patient Attrition Predictor**: AI models for identifying patients unlikely to return.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular feedback versus clinical roles.
- **Compliance Audit Master**: permanent logs for accrediting organization (JCI/URAC).
- **Global Settings & Globalization**: managing multi-regional survey standards.
