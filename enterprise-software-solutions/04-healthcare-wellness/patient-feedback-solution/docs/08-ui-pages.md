# 08 - Patient Feedback UI Pages

## 1. Quality Intelligence Hub (Dashboard)
- High-level KPIs: "Current NPS," "Total Feedback Volume," and "Case Resolution Speed."
- Live ticker showing the last 5 open-ended comments with color-coded sentiment.

## 2. Interactive Survey Builder
- Drag-and-drop interface for creating complex branching surveys.
- Previewer showing how the survey looks on iOS, Android, and Web.

## 3. Sentiment Exploration Bench
- Word-clouds and theme-bubbles for visual discovery of common patient complaints.
- "Deep Dive" tool to filter feedback by "Provider" or "Day of the Week."

## 4. Grievance Case Management Portal
- Kanban board for tracking open patient complaints from "New" to "Resolved."
- Integrated messaging interface for contacting the patient directly for resolution.

## 5. Staff "Shine" Portal (Recognition)
- A highly visual feed of positive patient comments that can be shared on breakroom monitors.
- "Applaud" button for managers to recognize staff mentioned in praise.

## 6. Regulatory Export Center
- One-click dataset generation for HCAHPS and insurance-specific quality metrics.
- Compliance tracker showing progress toward survey-volume benchmarks.
