# 02 - Patient Feedback Core Features

## 1. Multi-Channel Survey Engine
- **Transactional Surveys**: Automated triggers sent immediately after clinical check-out or discharge.
- **SMS & QR Integration**: One-click survey access via text message or posters in the waiting room.
- **Bedside Tablets**: Integrated kiosks for capturing feedback from admitted patients before they leave the facility.

## 2. Real-Time Sentiment Analysis
- **NLP Text Mining**: Automatically identifying specific themes (e.g., "Wait Time," "Staff Kindness," "Food Quality") from open-ended comments.
- **Emotion Detection**: Categorizing feedback by sentiment intensity to prioritize high-risk frustrations.
- **Keyword Trend Tracking**: Identifying emerging issues (e.g., "Cold Coffee") across multiple departments in real-time.

## 3. High-Priority Alerting & Workflow
- **"Red Alert" Notifications**: SMS/Push alerts to Quality Managers for any score below a specific threshold.
- **Automated Root-Cause Assignment**: Routing specific complaints to the relevant department head for resolution.
- **Feedback Loop Closure**: Tracking the time from "Complaint Received" to "Patient Contacted/Resolved."

## 4. Benchmarking & Comparative Analytics
- **Provider Performance Cards**: Individualized satisfaction scores for doctors and nurses compared to facility averages.
- **Departmental Heatmaps**: Comparative views of satisfaction across Cardiology, Pediatrics, ER, etc.
- **Regulatory Preview (HCAHPS)**: Predictive scoring for mandatory government-mandated surveys.

## 5. Anonymity & Secure Reporting
- **Trusted Anonymity**: Optional "Private Mode" allowing patients to report sensitive issues without fear of retaliation.
- **Patient Privacy Vault**: Securely storing contact info separately from feedback data to ensure compliance.
- **Staff Recognition Engine**: Automatically highlighting positive patient comments to boost staff morale.

## 6. Integration & Actionable Insights
- **EHR Linkage**: Mapping feedback to specific encounter IDs, visit reasons, and wait times for deep clinical context.
- **Action-Plan Tracker**: Integrated project management for quality teams to track facility improvements (e.g., "Upgrade ER Seating").
- **Closed-Loop Surveys**: Re-surveying patients after a resolution to measure "Recovery CSAT."
