# 13 - Patient Feedback Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Predictive Satisfaction AI**: Analyzing early clinic-visit data (e.g., wait time) to predict patient frustration before it happens.
- **Automated Video Feedback Analysis**: Using computer vision to analyze sentiment in short video-patient testimonials.
- **Hyper-Localized Quality Insights**: AI that suggests specific facility improvements (e.g., "Add more water stations in Section B") by reading patterns in thousands of comments.

## Ecosystem Growth
- **Global Patient Benchmark Network**: Allowing hospitals to compare their NPS scores anonymously against similar facilities worldwide.
- **Intelligent Staff Matching**: Suggesting staff-to-patient pairings based on historical satisfaction and communication-style compatibility.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Bedside Feedback**: "Pointing" at a hospital board to leave instant, location-specific feedback.
- **Voice-Activated Hospital Room**: "Alexa, tell the nurse manager I found the breakfast too cold."
- **Metaverse Quality Tours**: Using feedback to build "Digital Twins" of hospital facilities for virtual design testing.
