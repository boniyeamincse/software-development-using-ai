# 03 - Patient Feedback System Modules

## Survey Orchestrator & Channel Manager
Manages the distribution of surveys across SMS, Web, Email, and Kiosk.

## NLP Sentiment Discovery Engine
The intelligence layer that parses text feedback for themes, sentiments, and intent.

## Quality Manager Command Center
The primary interface for clinical leaders to monitor scores and manage alerts.

## Red-Alert & Case Management Hub
Facilitates the "Closed-Loop" workflow for resolving patient grievances.

## Analytics & Benchmarking Service
The heavy-duty reporting module for comparative and historical data analysis.

## Staff Recognition & Performance Portal
A motivating interface for frontline staff to see their impact and patient praise.

## Regulatory Reporting Vault
Generates the standardized datasets required for government and insurance quality audits.
