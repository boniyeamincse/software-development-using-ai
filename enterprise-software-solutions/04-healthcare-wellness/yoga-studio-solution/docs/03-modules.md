# 03 - Yoga Studio System Modules

## Class & Studio Orchestrator
The core engine for managing schedules, rooms, and instructor assignments.

## Student Pass & Billing Hub
Manages the lifecycle of memberships, class packs, and intro offers.

## Mobile Student Portal
The primary interface for students to book, pay, and track their practice.

## Teacher Workspace
A specialized tool for instructors to manage their classes and student interactions.

## Boutique POS & Inventory
Facilitates the sale of mats, gear, and supplies in the physical studio.

## Community & Engagement Feed
The social module for challenges, updates, and direct student-teacher messaging.

## Studio Growth Analytics
The reporting layer for monitoring revenue, attendance, and member retention.
