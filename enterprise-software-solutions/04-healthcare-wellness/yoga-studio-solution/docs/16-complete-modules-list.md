# 16 - Yoga Studio Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Practice & Pose Hub
- **Universal Yoga Master**: customized class types and a deep, multi-lingual pose library.
- **Student Consistency Hub**: tracking attendance, streaks, and practice intensity.
- **Teacher Roster Master**: managing instructor assignments and specializations.
- **Hybrid Session Bridge**: integrated support for both physical and virtual attendance.

## 2. Engagement & Mindfulness
- **Digital Journey Journal**: holistic student reflections on practice and intent.
- **Mind-Body Recommender**: (Optional) AI-driven suggestions based on mood and goals.
- **Community Event Hub**: specialized management for workshops, retreats, and YTT.
- **automated Prep Hub**: delivering guides and resources to registered students.

## 3. Operations & Facility
- **Precision Booking Master**: high-speed class and private session scheduling.
- **Teacher Substitution Hub**: managing last-minute roster changes efficiently.
- **studio inventory Hub**: (Optional) managing mats, props, and retail products.
- **Facility Access Hub**: (Optional) digital check-ins and studio door integration.

## 4. Finance & Membership Sync
- **Class-Pack & Recurring Billing**: flexible purchase options for diverse student needs.
- **Subscription Lifecycle Hub**: managing renewals, freezes, and cancellations.
- **Instructor Payout Master**: Calculating earnings based on attendance or flat-rates.
- **Financial Analytics Dashboard**: tracking the health of the studio business.

## 5. Interaction & Experience
- **Secure Teacher Messaging**: encrypted channel for personalized practice advice.
- **Student Performance Dashboard**: visualizing individual consistency and growth.
- **Feedback & Studio NPS**: automated collection of student experience and quality data.
- **AI Nudge & recovery Hub**: automated reminders for practice and wellness.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Student Privacy Hub**: high-security controls for goals and physical limitations.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular teacher versus manager permissions.
- **Immutable Transaction Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant logging for all financial activity.
- **Global Settings & Locales**: managing studio hours, holidays, and regional rules.
