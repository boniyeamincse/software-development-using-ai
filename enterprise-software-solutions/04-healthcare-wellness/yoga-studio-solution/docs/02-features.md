# 02 - Yoga Studio Core Features

## 1. Class & Workshop Orchestration
- **Intuitive Class Calendar**: Real-time display of Vinyasa, Yin, Meditation, and specialized workshops.
- **Series & Course Management**: One-click booking for multi-week progressions and teacher trainings.
- **Substitute Management**: Automated workflow for instructors to request and confirm class coverage.

## 2. Flexible Pass & Membership Engine
- **Class Pack Tracking**: Automatic deduction and refill alerts for 5/10/20 class bundles.
- **Recurring Unlimited Memberships**: Seamless monthly billing with student-controlled auto-pay.
- **Intro Offer Protection**: Smart logic to ensure "New Student" deals are only available to first-time visitors.

## 3. Seamless Student App
- **One-Tap Booking**: Mobile-first interface for reserving spots and managing waitlists.
- **Digital Check-In**: Students can check themselves in upon arrival via geofencing or QR.
- **My Practice History**: A visual journey of classes attended, teachers visited, and time spent on the mat.

## 4. Boutique Retail & POS
- **Mat & Prop Rental**: Quick tracking and billing for borrowed equipment.
- **Curated Retail Shop**: Modern POS for selling apparel, crystals, and beverages.
- **Account Credit**: Allowing members to charge retail items to their saved card on file.

## 5. Teacher & Community Hub
- **Teacher Portals**: Instructors can view their rosters, student notes, and payment summaries.
- **Private Messaging**: Secure, professional communication between students and teachers.
- **Social Newsfeed**: A studio-wide bulletin for sharing updates, events, and motivational prompts.

## 6. Financial & Retention Analytics
- **Utilization Heatmaps**: Identifying which classes are over-capacity and which need rebranding.
- **Churn Alerting**: Identifying students who haven't visited in 2 weeks for proactive outreach.
- **Reconciliation Dashboard**: Clean views of revenue across memberships, retail, and workshops.
