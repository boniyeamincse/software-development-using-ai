# 13 - Yoga Studio Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI Practice Companion**: Analyzing student photos of their "Warrior II" and providing gentle, digital alignment tips.
- **Smart Sequence Suggester**: Recommending specific classes based on the student's heart rate variability (HRV) and stress levels.
- **Intelligent Roster Optimizer**: Predicting workshop attendance weeks in advance to optimize marketing spending.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Connected Mat Integration**: Syncing with smart-mats to track weight distribution and balance during practice.
- **Global Studio Passport**: Allowing students to drop in at any partner studio worldwide with a single click.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Studio Assistant**: Allowing students to see the "Room Layout" and select their mat spot in augmented reality before arriving.
- **Mindfulness Metaverse Hubs**: High-fidelity, multi-user VR sound-baths and meditation sessions for remote students.
- **Voice-Activated Zen**: "Alexa, book my usual Yin Yoga class for tonight at 7 PM."
