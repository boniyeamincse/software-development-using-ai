# 16 - Hospital Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. clinical Excellence Core
- **Universal EHR Hub**: centralized, versioned health records for all patients.
- **Encounter & Note Master**: handling clinical documentation and ICD-10 tagging.
- **Order Entry (CPOE)**: specialized handling for medication, lab, and imaging orders.
- **Vitals & Observations**: real-time tracking of patient biometric data.

## 2. Scheduling & Resource Management
- **OT & procedure Scheduler**: strategic planning for operating theaters and staff.
- **Bed & Census Master**: Real-time visibility into hospital capacity and flow.
- **Staff Roster & Allocation**: matching clinical staff to departmental needs.
- **Appointment & Queue Hub**: managing outpatient flow across all clinics.

## 3. Operations & Logistics
- **Hospital Pharmacy Hub**: managing unit-dose inventory and medication safety.
- **Laboratory Information (LIS)**: (Optional) integration with lab equipment and results.
- **Blood Bank & procurement**: managing sensitive medical supply chains.
- **Support Services Hub**: managing housekeeping, dietary, and maintenance tasks.

## 4. Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)
- **patient Billing Factory**: Generating and tracking hospital service claims.
- **Insurance & Payer Bridge**: managing claims, denials, and authorizations.
- **Payments & Treasury Hub**: handling diverse patient and institutional payments.
- **Financial Analytics Controller**: analyzing profitability and revenue leakage.

## 5. Intelligence & Visibility
- **Executive Census Dashboard**: high-level metrics for hospital leadership.
- **AI Triage & symptom Shield**: Decision support for clinical prioritizations.
- **Patient Outcome Analytics**: tracking success rates and readmission metrics.
- **NBA Coordination Engine**: informing care managers on optimal next steps.

## 6. Security, Privacy & Admin
- **HIPAA Privacy & PHI Vault**: permanent logs of every health record access.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular clinical versus admin permissions.
- **Immutable clinical Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant storage for every medical order.
- **Regional Compliance Hub**: managing local healthcare laws and reporting rules.
