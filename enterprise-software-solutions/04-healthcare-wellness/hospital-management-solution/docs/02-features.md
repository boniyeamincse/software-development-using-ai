# 02 - Hospital Core Features

## 1. Unified Electronic Health Records (EHR)
- **Longitudinal History**: Complete history of patient encounters, allergies, and diagnoses.
- **Diagnostic Viewer**: Integrated display for DICOM images (X-rays, MRIs) and lab results.
- **Vital Sign Tracking**: Real-time integration with bedside monitors and wearable data.

## 2. Dynamic Bed & Ward Management
- **Live Occupancy Hub**: Real-time status of every bed across general wards, ICUs, and private rooms.
- **Patient Transfers**: Automated workflow for moving patients between departments with clinical handover.
- **Sterilization Tracking**: Monitoring the readiness of beds and operating rooms.

## 3. Clinical Workflow Automation
- **CPOE (Computerized Physician Order Entry)**: Standardized digital orders for medications and tests.
- **E-Prescribing**: Secure transmission of prescriptions to pharmacies with allergy-cross-check logic.
- **Surgical Scheduling**: Coordination of operating theaters, anesthesia teams, and specialized equipment.

## 4. Laboratory & Imaging Integration
- **LIS (Lab Information System)**: Automated sample tracking from collection to final report.
- **PACS Integration**: Direct access to high-resolution medical imaging within the patient chart.
- **Automated Alerts**: Immediate notification for "Critical Values" needing urgent attention.

## 5. Billing & Revenue Cycle
- **Point-of-Care Billing**: Clinical actions automatically trigger billing items to minimize leakage.
- **Insurance Orchestration**: Real-time eligibility checks and automated claim submission.
- **Financial Counseling**: Transparent cost estimation for patients before procedures.

## 6. Staff & Schedule Management
- **Specialist Roster**: Management of on-call schedules across multiple departments.
- **Shift Handover Logic**: Digital summaries for nursing shifts to ensure continuity of care.
- **Skills Tracking**: Ensuring only certified staff are assigned to specialized equipment or wards.
