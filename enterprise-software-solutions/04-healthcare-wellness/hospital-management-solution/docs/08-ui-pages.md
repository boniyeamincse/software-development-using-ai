# 08 - Hospital UI Pages

## 1. Unified Clinical Workbench (Doctor View)
- Dashboard showing current assigned patients with "High Alert" vitals highlighted.
- Tabbed interface: "Current Problems," "Active Meds," "Recent Results," and "Clinical Notes."
- Fast-action "Quick Order" buttons for common diagnostic panels.

## 2. Ward Control Center (Nursing View)
- Visual map of the ward with color-coded bed statuses (Occupied, Alert, Discharge-Pending).
- "Med Pass" checklist synchronized with electronic medicine cabinets.
- Real-time notification feed for patient call-bells and vital thresholds.

## 3. Imaging & Result Viewer
- High-fidelity medical image viewer with zooming and contrast adjustment tools.
- Side-by-side comparison of historical and current lab results with trend graphs.

## 4. Operation Theater Roster
- Timeline view of surgical slots across all rooms.
- Equipment status and sterile-supply readiness indicators.

## 5. Revenue & Billing Dashboard
- Real-time "Unbilled Revenue" tracker and insurance denial heatmap.
- Patient-facing self-service kiosk for check-in and payment.
