# 03 - Hospital System Modules

## Patient Admission & Registration (Front Office)
Handles appointments, trauma intake, and demographic data collection.

## Clinical EHR & Nursing Portal
The primary interface for doctors and nurses for charting, vitals, and progress notes.

## Operation Theater (OT) Management
Management of surgical schedules, equipment checklists, and anesthesia records.

## Pharmacy & Inventory Link
Tracks medical supplies, narcotics management, and automated stock reordering.

## Laboratory & Diagnostics Hub
Manages test samples, machine integrations, and diagnostic report generation.

## Revenue Cycle & Insurance Module
The financial hub for billing, payments, and multi-payer insurance claims.

## Facility & Bed Manager
The administrative layer for housekeeping, bed allocation, and ward oversight.
