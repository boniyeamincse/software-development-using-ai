# 13 - Hospital Roadmap

## AI & Future Clinical Tech
- **Predictive Deterioration AI**: Analyzing Vital Trends to predict "Sepsis" or "Cardiac Events" up to 6 hours before symptoms appear.
- **AI-Autonomous Scribing**: Voice-to-EHR transcription that builds clinical notes by listening to the doctor-patient encounter.
- **Radiology AI Co-pilot**: Automated first-pass scanning of X-rays to prioritize high-suspected fractures for immediate review.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Surgical Navigation**: Projecting MRI/CT scans onto the patient's body via AR glasses during complex procedures.
- **Hospital-at-Home Integration**: Extending the "HMS Ward" to a patient's house via wearable monitoring and telemedicine.
- **Digital Twin Simulation**: Using hospital data to build a digital simulation of patient flow to optimize staffing levels during flu seasons.

## Global Interoperability
- **Universal Patient Identity**: Cross-hospital identity resolution using biometric and cryptographic keys.
- **Blockchain Health Ledger**: Allowing patients to "Own and Carry" their records between different health systems globally.
