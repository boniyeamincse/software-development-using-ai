# 16 - Wellness Program Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Health & Biometric Core
- **Universal Wellness Profile**: central hub for health assessments and screenings.
- **Biometric Integrity Hub**: managing sensitive medical and physical measurement data.
- **precision Health Scorer**: algorithm-driven assessment of individual wellness.
- **Wearable Data Sync Bridge**: integrating with global ecosystem (Fitbit, Apple, Google).

## 2. engagement & Behavior Suite
- **Dynamic Challenge Master**: managing corporate and individual wellness competitions.
- **Leaderboard & Social Hub**: fostering community through real-time results.
- **Rewards & gamification Hub**: managing points, tiers, and badges for engagement.
- **Habit & Nudge orchestrator**: automated reminders and "nudges" for healthy behavior.

## 3. Coaching & Clinical Workspace
- **Secure Clinical Messaging**: (Optional) HIPAA-compliant channel for wellness coaching.
- **video consultation Master**: integrated area for synchronous coaching sessions.
- **Goal Commitment Hub**: collaborative tracking of individual wellness plans.
- **Professional Practice Hub**: specialized dashboard for wellness coaches.

## 4. Content & Wisdom Engine
- **Strategic Content Library**: managing wellness articles, videos, and meditations.
- **AI Recommendation controller**: personalized delivery of content based on health data.
- **Assessment & Survey Master**: building and deploying health risk assessments (HRA).
- **Curriculum & course Planner**: structured wellness training programs.

## 5. Analytics & Performance
- **Population Health Dashboard**: high-level visibility into organizational wellbeing.
- **Wellness ROI Analytics**: tracking the impact of programs on costs and productivity.
- **risk & Trend identifier**: identifying emerging health issues in the employee base.
- **Predictive health Hub**: AI models for identifying future chronic disease risks.

## 6. Security, Privacy & Admin
- **HIPAA Privacy & PHI Hub**: high-security controls for individual health data.
- **Aggregated Analytics Shield**: ensuring employers only see anonymized data.
- **Immutable Wellness Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant logging for all interactions.
- **Global Settings & Locales**: managing regional wellness rules and standards.
