# 13 - Wellness Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Personal Trainer**: Real-time workout correction and planning using mobile cameras and computer vision.
- **Predictive Mental Health**: Analyzing app engagement patterns to predict "Employee Burnout" and suggest proactive breaks.
- **Hyper-Personalized Nutrition AI**: Meal-planning based on the user's real-time metabolic data and local grocery availability.

## Modern Experiences
- **Metaverse Wellness Hubs**: Group yoga and meditation in high-fidelity VR environments.
- **Web3 Health Economy**: Users earning tradeable tokens for verifiable health improvements.
- **AR Health Walk**: Mapping "Digital Health Trails" in physical corporate offices to encourage movement during breaks.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Connected Kitchen Integration**: Syncing with smart fridges and ovens for automated nutritional tracking.
- **Family Wellness Plans**: Extending corporate benefits to the employee's entire household for a holistic health culture.
