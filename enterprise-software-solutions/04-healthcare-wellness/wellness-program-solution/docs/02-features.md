# 02 - Wellness Core Features

## 1. Gamified Health Challenges
- **Corporate Competitions**: Team-based step counts, sleep challenges, and hydration goals.
- **Leaderboards & Badges**: Real-time social recognition for health milestones and consistency.
- **Progressive Rewards**: Earning "Wellness Points" redeemable for insurance discounts or corporate perks.

## 2. Comprehensive Wearable Sync
- **Universal Connector**: Seamless integration with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and Garmin.
- **Activity Normalization**: Converting diverse data points (cycling, swimming, walking) into a single "Health Score."
- **Real-time Sync**: Background data ingestion to keep challenges up-to-date.

## 3. Health Risk Assessment (HRA)
- **Digital Health Surveys**: Clinical-grade questionnaires covering lifestyle, family history, and mental well-being.
- **Personalized Risk Scoring**: Quantitative feedback for users on their primary health focus areas.
- **Recommendations Engine**: Automated suggestion of programs (e.g., "Smoking Cessation") based on HRA data.

## 4. Coaching & Mental Health
- **Virtual Health Coaching**: Secure messaging and 1-on-1 video sessions with certified wellness coaches.
- **Mindfulness & Sleep Hub**: Integrated library of meditation tracks, sleep sounds, and stress-management guides.
- **Crisis Linking**: Rapid access to Employee Assistance Programs (EAP) and emergency mental health resources.

## 5. Preventative Milestone Tracker
- **Screening Reminders**: Automated notifications for annual physicals, dental checks, and age-specific screenings.
- **Vaccination Vault**: Secure storage and verification of vaccination records for corporate compliance.
- **Evidence Upload**: Users earn points by uploading proof of preventative health actions.

## 6. HR & Insurance Reporting
- **Incentive Management**: Automation of insurance premium discounts based on program participation.
- **Anonymized Aggregates**: High-level health heatmaps for HR to identify corporate-wide wellness trends.
- **Program ROI Analytics**: Measuring program cost vs. reduction in sick days and health claims.
