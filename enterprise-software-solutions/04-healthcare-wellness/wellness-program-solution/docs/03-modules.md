# 03 - Wellness System Modules

## Engagement & Gamification Engine
Manages the logic for challenges, leaderboards, points, and digital rewards.

## Wearable Integration Gateway
The specialized connector for ingesting and normalizing data from 100+ health devices.

## Health Assessment & HRA Portal
The clinical module for medical surveys and personalized risk scoring.

## Coaching & Counselor Workspace
The secure professional interface for health coaches to manage their client roster.

## Corporate HR Dashboard
The reporting layer for managing employee enrollments and monitoring anonymized trends.

## Rewards & Incentive Orchestrator
Syncs with payroll and insurance systems to distribute financial and corporate perks.

## Content & Media Library
The CMS for managing mindfulness audio, nutritional videos, and health education articles.
