# 16 - Clinic Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Practice Orchestration Hub
- **Universal Multi-Specialty Scheduler**: strategic planning for providers and resources.
- **Smart Queue & Wait-list Manager**: optimizing patient flow and throughput.
- **Patient Identity & Intake Hub**: streamlined onboarding and insurance verification.
- **Provider Roster & Locales**: managing staff across different clinic sites.

## 2. Clinical Workflow Suite
- **Dynamic SOAP Note Master**: template-driven clinical documentation with smart-phrases.
- **Order Management (Lab/Imaging)**: integrated requests with HL7/FHIR support.
- **results review Orchestrator**: managing the verification and notification of lab data.
- **Prescription & Rx Bridge**: (Optional) integration with external pharmacies.

## 3. Operations & Life-Cycle
- **Vaccine & Immunization Hub**: tracking pediatric and adult schedules with alerts.
- **Document Management Vault**: Secure storage for scans, referrals, and consents.
- **referral & Growth Bridge**: managing outbound and inbound patient referrals.
- **Inventory & Supply Hub**: managing clinical consumables and samples.

## 4. Revenue & Billing Sync
- **clinical Coding Engine**: converting encounters into ICD-10 and CPT coded claims.
- **Claim & Remittance Bridge**: managing the entire lifecycle of insurance payments.
- **Patient Financial Hub**: handling out-of-pocket payments and payment plans.
- **Billing Analytics Dashboard**: tracking DAR and claim denial trends.

## 5. Interaction & Engagement
- **Omni-channel Messaging Hub**: secure patient communication and reminders.
- **Patient Engagement Portal**: mobile-ready area for history, results, and booking.
- **Feedback & NPS Hub**: automated collection of patient satisfaction data.
- **Campaign & Outreach Hub**: targeted notifications for preventive care.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Immutable PHI Audit Vault**: tamper-proof logging of every medical record access.
- **HIPAA Privacy & Compliance Hub**: managing data visibility and staff roles.
- **terminall Health & Device Monitor**: securing hardware at the clinic front-desk.
- **Global Settings & Globalization**: managing multi-regional clinic rules.
