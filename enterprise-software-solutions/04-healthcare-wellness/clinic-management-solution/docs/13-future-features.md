# 13 - Clinic Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-SOAP Assistant**: Automatically suggesting Differential Diagnoses based on the "Assessment" text.
- **Intelligent Patient Triage**: Chatbots that rank patient urgency during online booking.
- **Predictive No-Show AI**: Identifying patients likely to miss appointments and proactively calling them for confirmation.

## Ecosystem Growth
- **Multi-Clinic "Corporate" View**: Specialized dashboards for owners with 10+ branch locations.
- **Integrated Marketplace**: Allowing patients to buy supplements or health wearables directly from the portal.

## Modern Experiences
- **Wearable Data Ingestion**: Doctors viewing real-time Apple Health/Fitbit data on the clinical chart.
- **One-Click Telehealth**: Transitioning an in-person visit to video with a single tap if the patient is stuck in traffic.
