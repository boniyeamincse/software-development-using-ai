# 08 - Clinic UI Pages

## 1. Practice Command Center (Dashboard)
- Daily overview: "Total Visits," "Revenue," and "Alerts" (Abnormal labs).
- "Waiting Room" ticker showing patient wait times and room assignments.

## 2. Interactive Calendar
- Multi-view (Day/Week/Month) with color-coding by visit type (Routine, New Patient, Procedure).
- Hover-cards for quick patient details without leaving the view.

## 3. Clinical Charting Workspace (EHR)
- Clean, focused interface for typing SOAP notes.
- Floating "Medication Tracker" and "Recent Lab Results" sidebar.
- "Order Tray" for instantly submitting prescriptions and lab requests.

## 4. Patient Engagement Hub
- Communication log: Every SMS, Email, and Portal Message sent to the patient.
- Automated reminder configuration (e.g., "Send SMS 24h before").

## 5. Billing & Claims Bench
- Table view of all "Pending Claims" with one-click resubmission for denials.
- Patient checkout modal with integrated card processing and email-receipt options.
