# 02 - Clinic Core Features

## 1. Smart Patient Scheduling
- **Multi-Doctor Calendar**: Drag-and-drop booking across multiple providers and rooms.
- **Online Booking Portal**: Patient self-service interface with real-time slot availability.
- **Automated Reminders**: SMS and Email notifications to reduce "No-Show" rates.

## 2. Optimized Clinical Charting (SOAP)
- **Specialty Templates**: Pre-built forms for Cardiology, Pediatrics, Dental, etc.
- **Voice-to-Text Integration**: Hands-free clinical documentation for faster consultations.
- **Historical Trends**: Visual comparison of vitals and lab results over multiple visits.

## 3. Integrated E-Prescribing & Pharmacy
- **Direct Submission**: Sending prescriptions directly to the patient's preferred pharmacy.
- **Drug Interaction Check**: Real-time safety alerts based on the patient's active medication list.
- **Medication Reconciliation**: Comparing current meds with new prescriptions to avoid errors.

## 4. Patient Engagement & Portal
- **Self-Service Access**: Patients can view their own lab results, summaries, and invoices online.
- **Secure Messaging**: HIPAA-compliant portal for non-urgent communication with the clinic staff.
- **Digital Intake Forms**: Pre-visit questionnaire submission to minimize waiting room time.

## 5. Simplified Practice Billing
- **One-Click Claims**: Automated ICD-10/CPT coding suggestions and paperless claim submission.
- **Integrated Payments**: Support for credit cards, Apple Pay, and payment plans at the front desk.
- **Financial Analytics**: Real-time tracking of revenue per provider and insurance aging reports.

## 6. Lab & Diagnostic Sync
- **Digital Requisitions**: Electronic orders sent directly to partner laboratories.
- **Automatic Result Ingestion**: Reports flow directly into the patient's digital chart with "Abnormal" flags.
- **Imaging Integration**: Basic viewer for X-rays and ultrasounds received from diagnostic centers.
