# 03 - Clinic System Modules

## Front Desk & Scheduling Hub
The administrative module for patient intake, check-in, and calendar management.

## Clinical Encounter Module (EHR Lite)
The core provider interface for SOAP notes, vitals, and surgical/procedural records.

## Billing & Insurance Orchestrator
Manages claim scrubbing, submission to clearinghouses, and patient invoicing.

## Engagement & Marketing Sync
Automated follow-ups, birthday wishes, and targeted promotional campaigns (e.g., flu shots).

## Inventory & Medical Supplies
Tracks clinical consumables, vaccines, and equipment maintenance schedules.

## Diagnostics Bridge
Integrates with external labs and imaging centers for automated result retrieval.

## Financial & Performance Insights
The reporting layer for practice owners to monitor growth and staff productivity.
