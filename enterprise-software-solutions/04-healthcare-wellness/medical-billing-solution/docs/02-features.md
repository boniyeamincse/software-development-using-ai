# 02 - Medical Billing Core Features

## 1. Multi-Payer Claim Orchestration
- **Universal Clearinghouse Sync**: Direct API integration with major clearinghouses (e.g., Change Healthcare, Waystar).
- **Electronic Claim Submission (837)**: Automated generation and transmission of HIPAA-compliant claim batches.
- **Real-Time Adjudication**: Immediate feedback from payers on claim acceptance or rejection for specific services.

## 2. Intelligent Claim Scrubbing
- **Rule-Based Engine**: Hundreds of pre-built checks for missing modifiers, invalid ZIP codes, and unbundled codes.
- **ICD-10/CPT Crosswalks**: Automated validation that the diagnosis code justifies the procedure code.
- **Denial Prediction**: AI-driven analysis flagging claims with a high probability of rejection before they are sent.

## 3. Revenue Cycle Automation (RCM)
- **Automated ERA Posting (835)**: Instant reconciliation of insurance payments and adjustments into the patient's ledger.
- **Secondary Billing**: Automated trigger of secondary insurance claims after the primary payer's portion is finalized.
- **Aging Management**: Smart worklists that prioritize unpaid claims based on "Days in AR" and dollar value.

## 4. Patient Financial Management
- **Digital Statements**: Clean, easy-to-read invoices delivered via email and secure SMS links.
- **Payment Portal**: Secure processing for credit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and automated payment plans.
- **Estimator Tool**: Predictive co-pay and deductible calculation for patients before they receive care.

## 5. Compliance & Audit Control
- **Audit-Ready Logs**: Immutable record of every claim change, resubmission, and payment adjustment.
- **OIG Scrutiny Checks**: Automated screening against excluded provider lists and fraudulent billing patterns.
- **HIPAA Financial Standards**: Strict adherence to the ASCA (Administrative Simplification Compliance Act).

## 6. Financial Analytics Dashboard
- **KPI Tracking**: Real-time visibility into Gross Collection Ratio, Days in AR, and Denial Rates.
- **Payer Performance**: Comparison of reimbursement speed and accuracy across different insurance companies.
- **Provider Productivity**: Analyzing billing volume and collection success per clinician.
