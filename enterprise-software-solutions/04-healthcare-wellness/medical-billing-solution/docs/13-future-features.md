# 13 - Medical Billing Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Driven Coding (CAC)**: Automatically suggesting CPT and ICD-10 codes by reading the clinical SOAP note text.
- **Smart Denial Recovery AI**: Predicting the most successful appeal strategy for a specific denial reason based on millions of past incidents.
- **Real-Time Patient Propensity Scoring**: AI modeling to predict which patients are most likely to need financial payment plans.

## Ecosystem Growth
- **Direct-to-Payer Blockchain integration**: Removing clearinghouse intermediaries for instant, verifiable claim settlement.
- **Global Payer Network**: One-click expansion for billing international insurance companies.

## Modern Experiences
- **Voice-Activated AR Billing Dashboards**: "Alexa, show me my highest-value denials from Blue Cross today."
- **Crypto-Collect Integrated Gateway**: Allowing patients to settle balances using regulated medical-currency stablecoins.
- **Self-Healing Scrubbing Rules**: A system that automatically updates its own rules based on new payer rejection patterns.
