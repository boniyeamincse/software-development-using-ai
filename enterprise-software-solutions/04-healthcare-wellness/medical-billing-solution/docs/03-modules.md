# 03 - Medical Billing System Modules

## Claim Preparation & Scrubbing Engine
The core logic layer that validates codes and prepares the 837 claim files.

## Clearinghouse & Payer Gateway
The communication module for transmitting batches and receiving real-time status updates.

## AR & Denial Management Hub
The interactive workspace for billing specialists to appeal denied claims and manage aging.

## Payment Postings & Reconciliation
Automates the ingestion of 835 ERA files and manual payment entry.

## Patient Billing & Portal Hub
Manages the generation of patient statements and the secure payment interface.

## Provider & Contract Manager
Stores reimbursement rates and contract rules for different insurance networks.

## Executive Financial Intelligence
The reporting and analytics suite for monitoring the total health of the revenue cycle.
