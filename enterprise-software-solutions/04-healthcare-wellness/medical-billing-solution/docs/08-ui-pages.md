# 08 - Medical Billing UI Pages

## 1. Billing Control Center (RCM Dashboard)
- High-level KPIs: "Daily Charges Submitted," "Net Collection Rate," and "Current AR Over 90 Days."
- "Action Required" ticker: High-value claims that were just denied.

## 2. Claim Scrubbing & Batching Console
- Interactive table showing all "Draft" claims with highlight icons for errors (e.g., "Missing DX Code").
- One-click "Bulk Fix" for common errors (e.g., updating a provider NPI across a batch).

## 3. Denial & Appeal Workbench
- Side-by-side view: "Denied Claim" vs. "Payer Rejection Code Explanation."
- Automated "Appeal Letter Generator" with relevant clinical evidence attachments.

## 4. Patient Billing & Statement Hub
- Visual timeline of the patient's financial journey for each visit.
- "Payment Plan" creator with sliders for adjusting monthly amounts and interest.

## 5. ERA Reconciliation Bench
- Split-screen view for manual reconciliation: "Reported Payer Amount" vs. "Expected Contract Amount."
- "Anomaly Flag" for payments that don't match the negotiated contract rate.
