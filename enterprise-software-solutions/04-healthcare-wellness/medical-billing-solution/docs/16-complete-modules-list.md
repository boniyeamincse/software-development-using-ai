# 16 - Medical Billing Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Claim Lifecycle Hub
- **Universal Claim Master**: central hub for EDI 837 and 835 transactions.
- **precision Coding Validator**: ensuring NPI, ICD-10, and CPT integrity.
- **Clearinghouse Bridge**: multi-payer integration for real-time filing.
- **Claim Status Monitor**: real-time tracking from submission to payment.

## 2. Insurance & Eligibility
- **real-time Eligibility Gate**: integrated verification of coverage and co-pays.
- **Payer Policy Master**: (Optional) managing specific rules and requirements per insurer.
- **Prior Authorization Hub**: tracking and managing medical necessity requests.
- **Coordination of Benefits (COB)**: managing primary, secondary, and tertiary payers.

## 3. Denial & Remediation
- **Denial Intelligence Hub**: automated analysis of ERA reason codes.
- **Remediation Task orchestrator**: structured workflows for correcting errors.
- **Appeal documentation Factory**: generating legal appeals with clinical support.
- **Denial Analytics Dashboard**: identifying trends in payer behaviors.

## 4. Patient Billing & Finance
- **Patient Statement Engine**: Generating clear, aggregated billing for consumers.
- **Payment & portal Bridge**: secure online payment and statement history.
- **Payment Posting Master**: automated reconciliation of EOBs with bank data.
- **Reimbursement & credit Hub**: managing refunds and balance adjustments.

## 5. Intelligence & Performance
- **Executive RCM Dashboard**: high-level visibility into DAR and revenue health.
- **Clean Claim Analytics**: tracking the efficiency of first-pass submissions.
- **Payer Performance Hub**: comparing reimbursement rates and times across insurers.
- **AI denial Predictor**: identifying high-risk claims before they leave the system.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Immutable Financial Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant logging for medical billing.
- **HIPAA PHI Protection Hub**: specialized encryption and masking for health data.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular billing versus clinical roles.
- **Regulatory Reporting Hub**: managing regional and national billing standards.
