# 03 - Mental Health System Modules

## Clinical Documentation & Note Engine
The core repository for specialized psychiatric records and mental status exams.

## Outcome Measurement & Screening Service
Manages the distribution, scoring, and visualization of therapeutic assessments.

## Secure Telehealth & Communication Hub
The encrypted layer for video therapy and HIPAA-compliant patient messaging.

## Crisis Safety & Escalation Manager
The priority module for monitoring high-risk indicators and managing safety plans.

## Treatment Planning & Goal Tracker
Facilitates long-term therapeutic planning and milestone monitoring.

## Behavioral Health Billing & Admin
Manages therapy-specific CPT coding, session authorizations, and insurance claims.

## Patient Wellness App
The mobile interface for clients to track mood, complete homework, and access support.
