# 02 - Mental Health Core Features

## 1. specialized Clinical Documentation
- **Dynamic Note Templates**: Specialized SOAP, DAP, and BIRP templates tailored for individual, group, and family therapy.
- **Mental Status Exam (MSE) Builder**: Structured tools for recording observations on mood, affect, and cognitive function.
- **Diagnosis (ICD-10/DSM-5) Sync**: Integrated lookup and coding for psychiatric conditions.

## 2. Integrated Teletherapy & Messaging
- **Secure Video Sessions**: Peer-to-peer encrypted video calls with integrated screen-sharing for therapeutic exercises.
- **Patient Messaging**: HIPAA-compliant asynchronous communication between clinicians and clients.
- **Digital Handouts & Homework**: Sending therapeutic resources and tracking patient engagement between sessions.

## 3. Outcome Tracking & Assessments
- **Automated Screening**: Scheduled delivery and scoring of standardized assessments (e.g., PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5).
- **Progress Visualization**: Interactive charts showing symptom trends over months or years.
- **Treatment Planning**: Collaborative goal-setting with automated reminders and milestone tracking.

## 4. Crisis Intervention & Safety
- **Safety Plan Management**: Collaborative creation of "Dynamic Safety Plans" accessible via the patient app.
- **Critical Alert System**: Real-time alerts for providers when assessment scores indicate high risk (e.g., Suicidality).
- **Embedded Crisis Resources**: One-click access to National Hotlines and local emergency services for the patient.

## 5. Group & Family Therapy Hub
- **Multi-Client Sessions**: Managing attendance and billing for group therapy and family interventions.
- **Shared vs. Private Notes**: Ability to keep individual-specific progress notes within a group clinical record.
- **Participant Portal**: Secure access for family members or guardians where clinically appropriate.

## 6. Billing & Insurance Orchestration
- **Behavioral Health E/M Coding**: Automated suggestion of CPT codes specific to therapy duration and complexity.
- **Superbill Generation**: Professional invoices for out-of-network patients to submit for reimbursement.
- **Auth Management**: Tracking insurance-approved session counts and expiration dates.
