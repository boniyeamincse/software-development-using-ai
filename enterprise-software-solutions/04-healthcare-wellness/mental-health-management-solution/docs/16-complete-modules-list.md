# 16 - Mental Health Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Clinical & Practice Core
- **Encrypted clinical Hub**: specialized storage for therapist notes and PHI.
- **Assessment & GAD/PHQ Engine**: managing standardized mental health tests.
- **Treatment Plan Master**: collaborative care planning between doctor and patient.
- **virtual consultation hub**: HIPAA-compliant video and audio bridge.

## 2. Privacy & Trust Suite
- **End-to-End Encryption Master**: ensuring all communications are private and secure.
- **Immutable PHI WORM Vault**: tamper-proof logging of every medical record access.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: (Optional) separating clinical versus administrative visibility.
- **Clinical Audit Logic**: permanent logging of session metadata and activity.

## 3. Patient Engagement & Journal
- **Digital Soul-Journal Master**: therapeutic area for patient reflections and history.
- **Sentiment & Mood Monitor**: tracking shifts in emotional health over time.
- **Mindfulness & CBT Content library**: integrated delivery of therapeutic resources.
- **AI Nudge & Intervention Hub**: automated suggestions based on patient input.

## 4. operations & Membership Sync
- **Precision Booking Master**: high-speed clinical and virtual scheduling.
- **Therapist Roster & session Hub**: managing provider availability and specialties.
- **Wait-list & Referral Orchestrator**: managing the intake and transition of patients.
- **System Settings & Globalization**: managing multi-regional clinical rules.

## 5. Finance & Billing Sync
- **Medical Billing Factory**: generating insurance and out-of-pocket claims.
- **Claims & Remittance Bridge**: managing the entire lifecycle of mental health billing.
- **patient Payment Hub**: handling session-based fees and payment plans.
- **financial Analytics Dashboard**: tracking the health of the clinical business.

## 6. Intelligence & Risk Hub
- **Executive Recovery Dashboard**: high-level visibility into patient outcomes.
- **AI Crisis Risk Detector**: real-time predictive models for identifying emergencies.
- **Treatment Outcome Analytics**: identifying high-impact therapeutic methods.
- **NBA Care Coordination Engine**: (Optional) informing care managers on optimal next steps.
