# 13 - Mental Health Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Therapeutic Linguistic Analysis**: AI that subtly flags changes in a patient's speech or writing patterns that might indicate a relapse (while maintaining 100% privacy).
- **VR Exposure therapy Integration**: Built-in modules for managing phobia-response therapy sessions in virtual environments.
- **Intelligent Crisis Prediction**: AI modeling that identifies "High Risk" weeks for patients by analyzing sleep and activity patterns from wearables.

## Ecosystem Growth
- **Global Provider Supervision Network**: Secure, cross-organization tools for therapists to receive clinical supervision anonymously.
- **Integrated Social Determinants (SDOH)**: Mapping local community resources (food, housing, legal) directly into the crisis response UI.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Grounding Tools**: In-app augmented reality "Calm Spaces" for patients during panic attacks.
- **Bio-Feedback Integration**: Real-time mirroring of heart rate and HRV durante therapy sessions to help clinicians teach regulation.
- **Voice-Activated Safety Plan**: "Alexa, I'm feeling overwhelmed - start my safety plan."
