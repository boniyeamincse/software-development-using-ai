# 09 - Mental Health System Workflows

## The Ongoing Therapy Workflow
1. **Appointment**: Patient attends a secure teletherapy session from their mobile app.
2. **Note Creation**: Therapist opens the SOAP note template; System pre-fills recent goals.
3. **Assessment**: During session, Therapist sends a "Digital GAD-7" to the patient's phone.
4. **Scoring**: Patient completes assessment; Score is immediately graphed on the clinician's screen.
5. **Signing**: After session, Therapist signs the note; encrypted blob is moved to the "Immutable Vault."
6. **Homework**: Therapist pushes a "Grounding Exercise" handout to the patient's app.

## The Crisis Escalation Workflow
1. Patient reports a "Suicidal Ideation" score on a routine screening assessment at 2 AM.
2. System identifies the high-risk score and the specific "Flagged Item."
3. **Emergency Alert**: Instant push notifications and SMS sent to the On-Call Crisis Team.
4. **Safety Plan**: Care team opens the latest "Safety Plan" to see the patient's agreed-upon coping skills.
5. **Human Contact**: Crisis Specialist initiates a secure call/video to the patient.
6. **Resolution**: Incident is marked "Resolved: Referred to ER" or "Resolved: Stabilized at Home."

## The Group Therapy Workflow
1. Group facilitator opens the "Anxiety Support Group" dashboard.
2. System displays all group members and their recent individual "Red Flags."
3. Facilitator marks attendance via the visual group seating chart.
4. Collaborative group summary is written; Individual-specific impacts are noted separately.
5. Billing system automatically generates individual charges for each participant.
