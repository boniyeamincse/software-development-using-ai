# 08 - Mental Health UI Pages

## 1. Clinician Workspace (Care Dashboard)
- High-level KPIs: "Daily Appointments," "Unsigned Notes," and "Active Patient Crisis Alerts."
- "Clinical Ticker" showing recent assessment scores with trend arrows (e.g., PHQ-9 decreasing).

## 2. specialized Note Entry Console
- Side-by-side view: "Last Session Summary" vs. "New SOAP Note Editor."
- Smart Checkboxes for Mental Status Exam (MSE) components.
- Integrated "Safety Plan" editor that highlights triggers.

## 3. High-Priority Alert & Crisis Bench
- Dedicated "War Room" UI for managing active crisis incidents.
- Collaborative chat for the care team during an emergency intervention.

## 4. Patient Wellness Mobile App
- Minimalist, calming design: "How are you feeling today?" mood tracker.
- "My Safety Plan" - Always available, even offline with large "Call Help" button.
- Secure library of handouts and prescribed meditation audio.

## 5. Group Therapy Management Portal
- Circular "Seating Arrangement" UI for tracking group dynamics and participation.
- Quick attendance tagging for large therapeutic groups.

## 6. Outcome Visualization Suite
- Heatmaps of patient progress across different modalities (CBT vs. DBT).
- Comparative data for Clinical Directors to see treatment efficacy per team.
