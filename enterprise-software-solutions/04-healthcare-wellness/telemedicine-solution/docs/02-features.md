# 02 - Telemedicine Core Features

## 1. High-Fidelity Virtual Visits
- **Low-Latency HD Video**: WebRTC-based video/audio optimized for varying network conditions.
- **Simultaneous Screen Sharing**: Providers can share diagnostic results or educational materials in real-time.
- **Multi-Party Sessions**: Support for including family members or medical translators in the call.

## 2. Secure Clinical Messaging
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Secure, persistent chat for non-urgent queries and follow-ups.
- **Automated File Transfer**: Secure exchange of photos (e.g., dermatology) and medical documents.
- **Notification Routing**: Escalation to on-call providers for urgent patient messages.

## 3. Virtual Waiting Room
- **Patient Self-Check-in**: Automated queue entry with real-time status updates for the patient.
- **Pre-Visit Surveys**: Capturing symptoms and reason-for-visit before the provider joins.
- **Interactive Content**: Educational videos or health tips for patients while "waiting."

## 4. Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)
- **Wearable Integration**: Real-time data capture from Apple Health, Google Fit, and Bluetooth medical devices (Oximeters, BP cuffs).
- **Threshold Alerts**: Automated notifications to clinical teams if a patient's home vitals cross safety limits.
- **Longitudinal Trend Views**: Visualizing home-monitored data within the clinical dashboard.

## 5. Integrated Documentation & EHR Sync
- **Side-by-Side Charting**: Documenting encounter notes directly within the video interface.
- **E-Prescribing**: Instant submission of medications to pharmacies during the call.
- **Automated Summaries**: Post-visit clinical summaries automatically shared to the patient portal.

## 6. Financial & Administrative Logic
- **Insurance Verification**: Automated check for telemedicine-specific coverage and co-pays.
- **Integrated Payments**: Secure processing of credit cards and digital wallets before the visit starts.
- **Compliance Logging**: Comprehensive session audit logs for HIPAA/GDPR regulatory proof.
