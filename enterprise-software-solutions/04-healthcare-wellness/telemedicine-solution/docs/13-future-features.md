# 13 - Telemedicine Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Vital Extraction**: Using the camera to detect Heart Rate and Respiration Rate via remote photoplethysmography (rPPG).
- **Automated Medical Captioning**: High-fidelity, real-time medical-grade subtitling for hearing-impaired patients.
- **AI Triage Companion**: Sentiment analysis of the patient's voice to detect pain or distress levels during the visit.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Connected Diagnostics**: One-click remote control for home medical robots or specialized cameras.
- **Global Specialist Exchange**: A borderless platform allowing patients in remote areas to consult with world-leading specialists instantly.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Clinical Overlays**: Allowing doctors to "Mark Up" the patient's video feed in 3D for surgical or physiotherapy guidance.
- **Voice-Activated Clinic**: "Alexa, start my virtual consultation with Dr. Smith."
- **Metaverse Consultations**: High-immersion VR therapy sessions for mental health and rehabilitation.
