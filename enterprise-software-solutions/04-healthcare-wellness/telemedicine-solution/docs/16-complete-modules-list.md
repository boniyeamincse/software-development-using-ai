# 16 - Telemedicine Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Virtual Consultation Hub
- **High-Fidelity Video Engine**: WebRTC-based low-latency audio/video core.
- **virtual Waiting room**: state-managed patient queue with priority triage.
- **Side-car Collaboration Hub**: integrated chat and file sharing during calls.
- **Session Record Master**: secure, automated recording and indexing of consultations.

## 2. Clinical & diagnostic Suite
- **Interactive medical EHR**: specialized view for providers during sessions.
- **Coded eRx Engine**: issue digital prescriptions with interaction checks.
- **multimedia Viewer Hub**: integrated viewer for lab results and imaging.
- **patient History Snapshot**: providing instant context to clinicians.

## 3. Interaction & Workflow
- **Precision Booking Master**: managing virtual-only and hybrid appointment slots.
- **automated Intake & Consent**: digital forms completed before the session starts.
- **Patient SMS/Push Alerts**: real-time notifications for appointment countdowns.
- **Provider Handover Hub**: (Optional) managing transitions between care teams.

## 4. Financial & Insurance Sync
- **telehealth Coding Factory**: Generating claims with required modifiers (GT, 95).
- **real-time Eligibility Gate**: validating insurance coverage before the call.
- **Patient Payment Hub**: handling co-pays and session-based fees.
- **Global Payer Bridge**: (Optional) direct integration with major insurers.

## 5. Intelligence & Performance
- **Executive NPS Dashboard**: tracking patient satisfaction and clinic health.
- **Provider Velocity Analytics**: analyzing session durations and outcomes.
- **AI Sentiment & Risk Shield**: detecting potential clinical risks in chats.
- **NBA Coordination Engine**: advising on follow-up and hospitalization needs.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **WORM Session Audit Vault**: tamper-proof logging of every virtual interaction.
- **clinical MFA Hub**: high-security access for PHI and health data.
- **HIPAA Compliance Monitor**: ensuring all communication is encrypted and secure.
- **Regional Health Bridge (HIE)**: syncing session data with national exchanges.
