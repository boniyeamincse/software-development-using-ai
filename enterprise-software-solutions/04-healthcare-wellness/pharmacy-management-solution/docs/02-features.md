# 02 - Pharmacy Core Features

## 1. Advanced Inventory & Cold Chain
- **Real-Time Stock Tracking**: Accurate visibility down to the individual pill/vial level.
- **Automated Expiry Alerts**: Proactive notifications for stock nearing end-of-life.
- **IoT Temperature Monitoring**: Live tracking for refrigerated medications with automated failure alerts.

## 2. Secure Dispensing Workflow
- **Barcode/QR Verification**: Triple-check logic: Doctor's order, stock bottle, and final pill pack.
- **Drug Interaction Engine**: Real-time cross-checking for Drug-Drug, Drug-Food, and Allergy conflicts.
- **Digital Label Generation**: Automated printing of dosage instructions, warnings, and patient identifiers.

## 3. Controlled Substance (Narcotics) Management
- **Electronic Logbook**: Permanent, immutable record of every narcotics transaction.
- **Double-Sign Approval**: Mandatory two-person verification for high-risk dispensing.
- **Inventory Reconciliation**: Daily automated counts and discrepancy reporting.

## 4. E-Prescribing & Insurance Sync
- **External Integration**: Receiving digital prescriptions from Surescripts, DoseSpot, or hospital EHRs.
- **One-Click Adjudication**: Real-time insurance eligibility check and automated claim submission.
- **Direct Pay Orchestration**: Secure processing for co-pays and cash-only patients.

## 5. Clinical Counseling & Patient Portal
- **Counseling Work-list**: Ensuring pharmacists provide mandatory consultation for specific medications.
- **Patient SMS Alerts**: Automated "Prescription Ready" and "Refill Reminder" notifications.
- **Digital Medication Guides**: Providing patients with QR codes for high-fidelity dosage videos and side-effect info.

## 6. Multi-Branch & Procurement
- **Centralized Purchasing**: Group-buying thresholds and automated vendor RFP generation.
- **Internal Transfers**: Easily moving stock between pharmacy branches to meet local demand.
- **Financial Dashboards**: Real-time gross margin and inventory turnover analytics.
