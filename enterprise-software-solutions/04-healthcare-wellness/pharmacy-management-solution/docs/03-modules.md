# 03 - Pharmacy System Modules

## Dispensing & Clinical Workbench
The primary interface for pharmacists and technicians to fill, check, and counsel prescriptions.

## Inventory & Warehouse Optimizer
Manages stock levels, multi-location transfers, and automated procurement logic.

## Narcotics Registry & Compliance
The high-security vault for tracking controlled substances and legal reporting.

## Insurance & Claims Orchestrator
The financial bridge for real-time adjudication and co-pay management.

## Patient Engagement Hub
Manages SMS notifications, portal access, and medication adherence trackers.

## Procurement & Vendor Portal
Facilitates bulk ordering, price-tracking, and vendor performance analytics.

## Clinical Intelligence Service
The background engine for drug-interaction checks and population health alerts.
