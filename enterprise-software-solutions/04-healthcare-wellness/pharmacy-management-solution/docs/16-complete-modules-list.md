# 16 - Pharmacy Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Inventory & Supply Chain
- **Universal stock controller**: Multi-dimensional tracking of medications and supplies.
- **precision Batch Hub**: managing lot numbers, serials, and expiry dates.
- **Supply Chain Bridge**: (Optional) automated re-ordering with major drug wholesalers.
- **Controlled Substance Master**: specialized workflows for highly regulated medications.

## 2. Clinical & safety Suite
- **Drug-Drug interaction Engine**: real-time checking against medication databases.
- **Patient Allergy Shield**: blocking dispensing for known patient-drug conflicts.
- **Clinical Review Hub**: pharmacist-led verification of e-prescriptions.
- **Medication Therapy Master**: (Optional) managing complex treatment regimens.

## 3. Dispensing & Operations
- **High-Speed Fill Master**: specialized UI for retail and institutional dispensing.
- **Three-Way Match Hub**: barcode-integrated verification for patient safety.
- **Label & Documentation Factory**: generating legal labels and patient medication info.
- **Inventory Adjustment Hub**: managing wastage, returns, and damages.

## 4. Financial & Insurance Sync
- **real-time Adjudication Hub**: managing instant claims with PBMs and insurers.
- **Point-of-Sale (POS) Bridge**: handling patient payments and co-pays.
- **Accounts Payable (AP) Hub**: managing supplier invoices and payments.
- **Billing Analytics Dashboard**: tracking DAR and claim denial trends.

## 5. Interaction & Engagement
- **Refill & Pickup Alert Hub**: omni-channel notifications for medication readiness.
- **POC Testing Hub**: managing clinical services like vaccines and rapid tests.
- **Patient medication Portal**: (Optional) mobile area for history and refill requests.
- **Doctor Collaboration Bridge**: managing refill authorizations and order queries.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Immutable Narco Audit Vault**: WORM-compliant logging for controlled substances.
- **centralized RBAC Controller**: defining granular pharmacist versus assistant roles.
- **Regulatory Reporting Hub**: managing regional reporting requirements.
- **Pharmacy Settings & Locales**: managing store-specific rules and hours.
