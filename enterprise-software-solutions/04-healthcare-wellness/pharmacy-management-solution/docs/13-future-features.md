# 13 - Pharmacy Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Predictive Inventory**: Predicting "Regional Outbreaks" (e.g., flu) to stock specific medications before demand spikes.
- **Computer Vision Verification**: Using cameras to automatically count pills and verify shape/color/markings against the drug master.
- **Personalized Genomic Pharmacy**: Tailoring dosage and medication choices based on the patient's genetic response markers.

## Modern Experiences
- **Drone Delivery Integration**: Automated dispatch of urgent medications from central hubs to remote patient locations.
- **AR Pharmacy Assistant**: Helping technicians find stock on shelves via AR-guided glasses.
- **Self-Service Vending Integration**: Secure, biometric pill-vending kiosks for non-narcotic refills.

## Global Logistics
- **Blockchain Track-and-Trace**: Verifying the total provenance of a drug from factory to patient to prevent counterfeits.
- **Universal Multi-Payer API**: One-click integration with every insurance and government payer globally.
