# 01 - Expense Management Overview

## Introduction
The **Expense Management Solution** is an enterprise-grade financial tool designed to automate the capture, approval, and reimbursement of business expenses. It transforms the often tedious "Receipt-to-Reimbursement" process into a streamlined, digital workflow.

## The Spending Challenge
Disorganized business spending leads to multiple organizational pain points:
- **Receipt Loss & Fraud**: Manual processes often result in lost documentation or duplicate filings.
- **Administrative Delays**: Finance teams spending excessive time manually data-entering and verifying receipts.
- **Policy Non-Compliance**: Difficulty in enforcing corporate travel policies and spending limits.
- **Tax & Audit Risk**: Incomplete records causing issues during tax filing or external financial audits.

## The Solution
A cloud-native expense orchestrator featuring AI-powered receipt scanning, real-time policy validation, and seamless integration with corporate credit cards and accounting systems. It empowers employees with mobile capture tools while providing finance leaders with total visibility and control over company spend.

## Core Objectives
- **Automated Capture**: Using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract data from receipts instantly.
- **Policy Enforcement**: Real-time alerts for out-of-policy spending at the point of submission.
- **Rapid Reimbursement**: Accelerating the cycle from expense submission to bank deposit.
- **Actionable Insights**: Advanced analytics for optimizing travel budgets and vendor negotiations.
