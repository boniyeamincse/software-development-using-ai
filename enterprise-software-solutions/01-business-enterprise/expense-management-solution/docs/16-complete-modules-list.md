# 16 - Expense Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Digitization & Capture Hub
- **Intelligent OCR Engine**: extracting data from receipts, invoices, and bills.
- **Mobile Evidence Hub**: offline-first application for on-the-go expense capture.
- **Evidence Formatting Bridge**: normalizing image quality and formats for processing.
- **Merchant & Category Matcher**: automatically tagging expenses based on history.

## 2. Policy & Compliance Suite
- **Global Policy Engine**: enforcing corporate spending rules across departments.
- **Currency & Tax Hub**: handling real-time exchange rates and VAT/GST recovery.
- **per-Diem Manager**: calculating daily allowances based on location and role.
- **Conflict & Duplicate Shield**: preventing double-claims and overlaps.

## 3. workflow & Approval Suite
- **Tiered Approval Orchestrator**: structured submission and validation workflows.
- **Approval Proxy Hub**: (Optional) allowing admins to manage expenses for execs.
- **escalation & SLA Monitor**: driving efficiency in the reimbursement lifecycle.
- **Mobile Approval Hub**: enabling managers to approve claims via push notifications.

## 4. Financial & Payment Sync
- **Reimbursement Gateway**: triggering direct payments via bank or payroll bridges.
- **Corporate Card Hub**: real-time sync with enterprise credit card providers.
- **Batch Disbursement Utility**: handling high-volume payments and SEPA files.
- **ERP & Accounting Bridge**: exporting verified expenses to the general ledger.

## 5. Intelligence & Visibility
- **Executive Spend Analytics**: high-level visibility into company-wide costs.
- **Category & Vendor ROI Tracker**: identifying opportunities for volume discounts.
- **Internal Benchmarking Hub**: comparing departmental costs and policy adherence.
- **Custom Policy Architect**: tools for creating and testing new spending rules.

## 6. Security, Trust & Admin
- **Immutable Expense Audit Vault**: Tamper-proof logging for every submission.
- **AI Fraud & Anomaly Shield**: detecting suspicious patterns in financial claims.
- **GDPR & Privacy Controller**: managing sensitive PII and financial records.
- **Entity & Department settings**: managing multi-national organizational rules.
