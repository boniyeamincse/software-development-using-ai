# 02 - Expense Core Features

## 1. AI-Powered Mobile Capture
- **Rapid Scanning**: Automatic extraction of Vendor, Amount, Date, and Tax from photos.
- **Email Ingestion**: Forward digital receipts directly to a unique expense email address.
- **Direct Card Sync**: Real-time feed of transactions from corporate VISA/Mastercard/Amex profiles.

## 2. Dynamic Policy Engine
- **Custom Spend Categories**: Different limits for "Meals," "Lodging," "Travel," and "Entertainment."
- **Policy Guardrails**: Automated flagging of expenses exceeding per-diem or project-specific limits.
- **Mandatory Fields**: Conditional requirements (e.g., "Receipt required for all items > $25").

## 3. Intelligent Approval Workflows
- **Multi-Level Approval**: Team Manager -> Department Head -> Finance.
- **Conditional Routing**: Automatically escalate high-value expenses or out-of-policy items.
- **Threaded Communication**: Disputing and correcting specific line items within a report.

## 4. Multi-Currency & Global Tax
- Automatic conversion and exchange rate tracking at the time of expense.
- VAT/GST extraction and categorization for international tax recoverability.
- Global per-diem rate support for international travel.

## 5. Seamless Reimbursement
- Integrated bank transfer (ACH/Wire) for direct-to-employee deposits.
- Payroll sync for processing reimbursements alongside monthly salaries.
- Support for "Third-Party Reimbursement" (Billing expenses directly to clients).

## 6. Spend Analytics & Reporting
- Visual dashboards showing spend by Category, Department, or Individual.
- Trend analysis identifying high-cost vendors for potential volume negotiations.
- Fraud detection alerts for overlapping receipts or unusual spending patterns.
