# 03 - Expense System Modules

## Expense Capture Suite
The mobile and web interfaces where employees upload receipts and create expense line items.

## Policy & Compliance Engine
The central logic module that stores and enforces corporate spending rules and tax requirements.

## Approval Orchestrator
The workflow engine that routes expense reports through the appropriate management hierarchy.

## Finance & Reimbursement Hub
The administrative workspace for processing bank transfers, payroll sync, and accounting exports.

## Cards & Integration Bridge
Manages the real-time feeds from corporate credit cards and external financial institutions.

## Analytics & BI Portal
The executive-level dashboard for monitoring company-wide spending trends and budget health.
