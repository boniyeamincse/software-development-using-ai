# 13 - Expense Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Fully Autonomous Approvals**: High-precision AI that automatically approves 95% of in-policy, card-matched expenses.
- **Real-Time Tax Recovery AI**: Identifying and filing for international VAT recovery automatically based on high-res receipt scan metadata.
- **Conversational Spend Assistant**: "Alexa, add my airport parking receipt to my London trip report."

## Ecosystem Integration
- **Direct Rideshare Sync**: Partnerships with Uber/Lyft for automated, receipt-less expense creation.
- **Travel Concierge**: Integrated booking engine that enforces policy at the moment of flight/hotel purchase.

## Modern Compliance
- **Blockchain Receipt Verification**: Partnering with major vendors for encrypted, digital receipts provided directly via smart-contracts to eliminate fraud.
- **ESG Spend Tracking**: Tracking the carbon footprint of corporate travel and suggest ECO-offsets during the reimbursement phase.
