# 02 - Accounting Core Features

## 1. Multi-Entity Double-Entry Ledger
- Global Chart of Accounts (COA) with customizable segments.
- Support for multiple subsidiaries and inter-company eliminations.
- Real-time trial balance and ledger reconciliation.

## 2. Accounts Receivable (AR) & Billing
- Professional invoicing with automated payment reminders.
- Credit memo management and bad-debt tracking.
- Customer age-analysis (Aged Receivables) reports.

## 3. Accounts Payable (AP) & Procurement Sync
- Vendor invoice ingestion and 3-way matching (PO, Receipt, Invoice).
- Support for ACH, Wire, and Check payment workflows.
- Expense reimbursement and corporate card reconciliation.

## 4. Banking & Reconciliation
- Direct bank feeds for automated transaction importing.
- AI-driven transaction categorization and matching logic.
- Managed bank reconciliation with instant discrepancy alerts.

## 5. Tax & Compliance Orchestration
- Dynamic tax calculation for 100+ jurisdictions.
- Automated generation of tax returns and localization reports.
- Fixed asset management with automated depreciation schedules.

## 6. Financial Planning & Analytics
- Budget creation with "Budget vs. Actual" variance analysis.
- Multi-dimensional reporting (by department, project, or location).
- Predictive cash-flow forecasting based on AR/AP trends.
