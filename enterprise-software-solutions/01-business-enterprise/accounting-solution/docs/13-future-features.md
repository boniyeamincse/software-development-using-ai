# 13 - Accounting Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Autonomous Bookkeeping**: Natural language processing that automatically categorizes 99% of bank transactions without human intervention.
- **Predictive Anomaly Detection**: AI that scans the ledger for patterns indicative of internal fraud or double-billing.
- **Voice-Activated Analytics**: "What was our net margin for the hardware project last quarter?"

## Ecosystem Integration
- **Direct Government Integration**: Seamless, one-click tax filing and document submission to national revenue services.
- **Embedded Financing**: AI that analyzes the company’s books to offer pre-approved working capital loans directly in the dashboard.

## Modern Experiences
- **Real-Time Global Consolidation**: Dynamic, per-second financial statements for massive multinational corporations.
- **Environmental Ledgering**: Tracking carbon credits and sustainability metrics alongside financial items as a "Sustainability Ledger."
