# 03 - Accounting System Modules

## General Ledger Core
The foundational module where every financial transaction is recorded, validated, and balanced.

## Revenue & AR Workspace
Focuses on income tracking, customer billing, and payment collection.

## Purchasing & AP Hub
Manages all outgoing expenses, from vendor bills to utility payments.

## Cash & Bank Manager
Handles bank connectivity, cash positioning, and reconciliation work.

## Tax & Fixed Assets Module
Specialized logic for handling complex tax calculations and long-term asset depreciation.

## Reporting & BI Suite
The visualization layer for generating compliant financial statements and management dashboards.
