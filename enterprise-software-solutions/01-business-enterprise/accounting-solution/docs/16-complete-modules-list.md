# 16 - Accounting Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. General Ledger & Integrity
- **Double-Entry Transaction Hub**: high-fidelity core for all financial movements.
- **Multi-Currency Orchestrator**: Handling global exchange rates and revaluations.
- **Chart of Accounts (COA) Master**: customizable structure for entire organization.
- **Financial Period Manager**: handling month-end and year-end closing processes.

## 2. Accounts Receivable (AR)
- **Invoice & Credit Factory**: Generating and tracking customer billing.
- **Aging & Collections Engine**: automated reminders and tracking for unpaid dues.
- **Customer Statement Portal**: Secure area for clients to view and pay invoices.
- **Revenue Recognition Shield**: (Optional) managing compliance with IFRS-15.

## 3. Accounts Payable (AP)
- **Vendor & Payee Hub**: managing supplier invoices and payment terms.
- **Purchase Bill Orchestrator**: automated handling of inward financial claims.
- **Approval Workflow Engine**: tiered authorization for large bill payments.
- **Payment Batching Utility**: handling bulk bank transfers and checks.

## 4. Banking & Treasury
- **Bank Statement Bridge**: importing and reconciling data from global banks.
- **Cash Flow Monitor**: real-time tracking and prediction of liquidity.
- **Internal Fund Transfer**: managing movements between company accounts.
- **Credit Card Expense Hub**: tracking and categorizing corporate card spend.

## 5. Asset & Tax Management
- **Fixed Asset Tracker**: managing depreciation and lifecycle of company assets.
- **Universal Tax Service**: Calculating VAT, GST, and Sales tax per region.
- **Tax Filing Generator**: Preparing data for regional regulatory submissions.
- **Employee Expense Manager**: integrated workflow for claims and receipts.

## 6. Intelligence, Audit & Admin
- **Financial BI Dashboard**: Real-time visualization of P&L and Balance Sheets.
- **AI Fraud & Anomaly Shield**: detecting suspicious patterns in the ledger.
- **Tamper-proof Audit Vault**: Immutable logging of every financial interaction.
- **Budget & Variance Hub**: strategic comparison of plans versus actuals.
