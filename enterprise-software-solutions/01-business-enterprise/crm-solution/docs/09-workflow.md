# 09 - CRM System Workflows

## The Sales Lifecycle (Lead to Close)
1. **Ingestion**: A whitepaper download triggers a new Lead creation.
2. **Qualification**: AI lead scorer assigns a points; Sales rep contacts Lead.
3. **Conversion**: Lead converted to Contact + Account + Opportunity.
4. **Nurturing**: Automated email sequence keeps the brand top-of-mind.
5. **Negotiation**: Rep generates Quote; Opportunity moves to "Proposal Sent."
6. **Closing**: E-signature received; Deal marked "Closed Won"; ERP notified for billing.

## Lead Assignment Workflow
1. New Lead arrives from "Enterprise" sector in "California."
2. System checks Sales Team availability and territory mapping.
3. Lead assigned to "John Doe"; Slack notification sent instantly.
4. If "John Doe" doesn't open the lead in 4 hours, it is automatically re-assigned.
