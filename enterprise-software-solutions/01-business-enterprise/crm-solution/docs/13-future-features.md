# 13 - CRM Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Generative Sales Assistant**: AI that drafts personalized follow-up emails based on the context of previous calls.
- **Sentiment Analysis on Calls**: Real-time mood detection for VoIP calls to alert managers of difficult conversations.
- **Predictive Lead Scoring 2.0**: Using deep learning to identify "Hidden Gems" in the database.

## Collaborative Selling
- **Shared Slack Channels**: Automated setup of shared Slack channels for high-value deal negotiation.
- **VR Presence Meetings**: Integration with VR platforms for immersive remote product demos.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **One-Click ERP Sync**: Deep, bi-directional sync with production and shipping systems for real-time delivery updates in the CRM.
- **Marketplace App Store**: Allowing partners to build custom widgets and plugins for the contact sidebar.
