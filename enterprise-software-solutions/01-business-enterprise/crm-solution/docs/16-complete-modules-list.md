# 16 - CRM Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. relationship Management Core
- **Advanced Lead Controller**: specialized handling for initial queries and qualification.
- **Contact & person Hub**: Centralized profiles with rich enrichment from social sources.
- **Account & Org Hierarchy**: Managing complex B2B relationships and parent-child companies.
- **Timeline Activity aggregator**: Unified view of all touchpoints across email, call, and chat.

## 2. Sales Lifecycle & Operations
- **Dynamic Pipeline Manager**: Kanban-based deal tracking with custom stages.
- **Sales sequence Automator**: automated multi-touch drip campaigns for prospecting.
- **Quote & CPQ Engine**: Configure, Price, Quote tools with automated PDF generation.
- **Territory & Tier Allocator**: assigning accounts to reps based on region and value.

## 3. Communication & Engagement
- **Mail Sync Bridge**: real-time 2-way sync with Gmail and Office 365.
- **VoIP & SMS Gateway**: Integrated calling and messaging within the interface.
- **Meeting Scheduler Hub**: Calendly-style booking links for sales reps.
- **Customer Success Portal**: specialized view for account management post-sale.

## 4. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Suite
- **Predictive Lead Scoring**: AI-driven prioritization based on historical success.
- **Sales Intelligence Bot**: real-time assistance and "Next Best Action" tips.
- **Conversational Analytics**: analyzing call transcripts for sentiment and coaching.
- **Forecaster AI**: Deep learning models for accurate revenue prediction.

## 5. Operations & Intelligence
- **Executive Revenue Dashboard**: High-level tracking of north-star sales metrics.
- **Campaign ROI Tracker**: attributing deals to specific marketing initiatives.
- **Commission & Payout Hub**: automated calculation of sales incentives.
- **Data Integrity Monitor**: automated cleaning and de-duplication of records.

## 6. Security, Privacy & Admin
- **Global RBAC controller**: defining granular permissions for sales teams.
- **GDPR & Privacy Guard**: managing data subject requests and transparency.
- **Audit & Compliance Vault**: permanent logs of every deal and data movement.
- **Integration Marketplace Hub**: managing connections with ERP, Slack, and others.
