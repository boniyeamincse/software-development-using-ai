# 03 - CRM System Modules

## Sales Pipeline Orchestrator
The primary workplace for sales teams to manage deals, track milestones, and view their individual targets.

## Contact Intelligence Hub
The central repository for account and contact data, featuring enriched profiles and historical interaction logs.

## Communication & Engagement Module
Handles the integration of Email, VoIP, and SMS to ensure all customer dialogue is captured and logged.

## Analytics & Forecasting Center
The high-level reporting module for management to visualize revenue trends and territory performance.

## Integration & Automation Bridge
The "Engine Room" where workflows are defined and connections to external ERP, Marketing, and Support tools are managed.
