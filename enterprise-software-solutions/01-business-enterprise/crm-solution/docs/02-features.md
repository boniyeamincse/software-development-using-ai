# 02 - CRM Core Features

## 1. Lead & Opportunity Management
- Multi-channel lead ingestion (Web forms, Email, Social Media).
- Customizable sales stages and "Drag-and-Drop" pipeline management.
- Automated lead scoring based on demographic and behavioral data.

## 2. Contact & Account Management
- Hierarchical account structure (Parent/Child companies).
- Unified activity feed: Emails, calls, meetings, and notes in one place.
- LinkedIn and social profile integration for enhanced contact data.

## 3. Sales Force Automation
- Automated follow-up sequences (Sequences/Cadences).
- Quote and Proposal generator with e-signature integration.
- Intelligent lead assignment rules based on territory, industry, or workload.

## 4. Multi-Channel Communication
- Integrated email client with tracking (Opens, Clicks).
- Built-in VoIP dialer with automated call logging and recording.
- Professional SMS and WhatsApp messaging capabilities.

## 5. Reporting & Sales Forecasting
- Real-time dashboards for individual and team performance.
- Predictive forecasting based on historic win rates and pipeline velocity.
- Custom report builder with scheduled delivery.

## 6. Marketing Integration
- Seamless sync with marketing automation tools for "Lead-to-Revenue" tracking.
- Campaign attribution: Identifying which marketing efforts drove specific deals.
