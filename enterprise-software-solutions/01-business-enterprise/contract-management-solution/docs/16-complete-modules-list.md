# 16 - Contract Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Authoring & Template Hub
- **Universal Template Master**: centralized control over standard corporate agreements.
- **Dynamic Clause Library**: managing approved legal language and alternatives.
- **Contract generation Engine**: automated creation of drafts with variable data.
- **Collaborative Editor Suite**: real-time authoring with redlining and history.

## 2. Negotiation & workflow
- **Negotiation orchestrator**: managing the back-and-forth between internal/external.
- **External Signatory Portal**: Secure area for counterparties to review and edit.
- **multi-Tier Approval Hub**: Enforcing legal, financial, and executive reviews.
- **Version Control Master**: tracking every iteration and redline change.

## 3. Execution & Trust
- **E-Signature Bridge**: real-time sync with DocuSign, HelloSign, and others.
- **Immutable Executed Vault**: tamper-proof storage for finalized agreements.
- **Audit logic Hub**: permanent logging of every approval and signature step.
- **Execution certificate Hub**: managing digital fingerprints and certificates.

## 4. Post-Execution lifecycle
- **Obligation & Milestone Tracker**: ensuring compliance with contract terms.
- **Renewal & Expiry Monitor**: automated alerts and renewal workflow triggers.
- **Compliance & Deviation Hub**: flagging contracts that vary from standard clauses.
- **Legal Entity Mapping**: associating contracts with specific corporate subsidiaries.

## 5. Intelligence & Visibility
- **Contract Portfolio Dashboard**: high-level visibility into TCV and exposure.
- **AI clause Risk Shield**: predictive identification of unfavorable terms.
- **Negotiation Velocity Analytics**: identifying bottlenecks in the legal process.
- **custom Clause Architect**: tools for legal teams to build and test templates.

## 6. Security, Governance & Admin
- **centralized RBAC Hub**: separating legal, sales, and external access.
- **Global Compliance Audit log**: permanent record of the entire contract history.
- **Data Privacy Guard**: managing sensitive commercial and personal info.
- **System Settings & Globalization**: managing multi-regional legal standards.
