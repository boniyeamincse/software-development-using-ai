# 13 - Contract Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Autonomous Negotiator**: ML models that can automatically accept or suggest pre-approved redlines during the first pass of a negotiation.
- **Semantic Obligation Discovery**: AI that scans all legacy PDFs to find "Hidden Commitments" that weren't tagged during ingestion.
- **Voice-Activated Contract Retrieval**: "What are the termination terms for the Acme Corporation MSA?"

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Direct Integration with Smart Contracts**: Automatically triggering blockchain-based payments based on contract milestones.
- **Universal Legal Directory**: Integration with government registries to automatically update party tax IDs and statuses.

## Modern Experiences
- **AR Redlining**: Visualizing contract changes in a collaborative virtual meeting space.
- **Low-Code Workflow Builder**: Non-technical legal staff can build "Drag-and-Drop" approval loops for new contract types.
