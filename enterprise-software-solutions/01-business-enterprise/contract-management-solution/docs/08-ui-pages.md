# 08 - Contract UI Pages

## 1. Collaborative Drafting Workbench
- Split-screen View: Draft on the left, Clause Playbook on the right.
- High-fidelity PDF/Docx renderer with "Comment Bubbles" for redlining.

## 2. Integrated Negotiation Portal (Counter-party View)
- Minimalist interface for external parties to view, edit, and message the internal legal team.
- Version comparison tool showing "What changed since our last draft."

## 3. Executive Contract Dashboard
- Visual visualization of "Contract Throughput Speed" and "Total Contract Value."
- "Hot Map" of jurisdictions with active agreements.

## 4. Obligations Calendar
- Interactive timeline showing all upcoming renewals and service milestones.
- Drag-and-drop reassignment of obligation owners.

## 5. Intelligent Global Search
- Amazon-style facets for filtering by Value, Date, Party, or Legal Term.
- Highlighted text snippets within search results for quick verification.
