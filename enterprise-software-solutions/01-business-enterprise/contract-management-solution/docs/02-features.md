# 02 - Contract Core Features

## 1. Smart Contract Authoring
- **Template Library**: Pre-approved master agreements (MSA), NDAs, and SLAs.
- **Clause Playbook**: A library of "Best Alternative" clauses for rapid negotiation adjustment.
- **Microsoft Word Integration**: Full bi-directional sync with familiar legal drafting tools.

## 2. Collaborative Negotiation & Redlining
- **Real-Time Track Changes**: Multi-user redlining with conflict resolution logic.
- **Version Control**: Permanent history of every draft, comment, and amendment.
- **Secure External Portal**: Invite counter-parties to negotiate within the platform.

## 3. Integrated E-Signature
- **Multi-Provider Support**: Built-in support for DocuSign, Adobe Sign, or internal signing logic.
- **Signer Sequencing**: Logic for ensuring "Internal Approval" before "Client Signature."
- **Immutable Evidence**: Legally binding audit logs of the signing event.

## 4. AI-Driven Contract Analysis
- **Automatic Extraction**: AI identifying Parties, Dates, Amounts, and Termination Clauses.
- **Risk Flagging**: Automated alerts for "Automatic Renewal" or "Limitation of Liability" terms.
- **Legacy Migration**: Mass-ingestion tool to digitize and tag thousands of historical PDF contracts.

## 5. Obligation & Milestone Tracking
- **Proactive Alerts**: Notifications for upcoming renewal dates and termination windows.
- **Compliance Linkage**: Mapping contract terms directly to operational compliance controls.
- **Revenue/Spend Link**: Integration with Accounting to track contract values.

## 6. Centralized Search & Repository
- **Full-Text OCR Search**: Find phrases across every PDF and Docx in the repository.
- **Dynamic Metadata Filters**: Filter by Expiry Date, Jurisdiction, or Counter-party.
- **Hierarchical Linking**: Link Amendments and Statements of Work to their Master Agreement.
