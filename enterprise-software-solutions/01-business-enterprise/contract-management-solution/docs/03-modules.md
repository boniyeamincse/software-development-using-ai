# 03 - Contract System Modules

## Drafting & Template Portal
The creative studio for building and maintaining the enterprise's legal templates and clause libraries.

## Negotiation Workspace
The collaborative environment for internal and external parties to redline and finalize documents.

## E-Signature Orchestrator
The module that manages the signature workflow, vendor integration, and legal verification.

## Contract Repository & Vault
The high-security, searchable storage for all finalized and historical agreements.

## AI Analytics Engine
The background logic that extracts data from documents and identifies legal risks.

## Milestone & Obligation Engine
The proactive logic layer that monitors contract dates and triggers notifications.
