# 04 - Contract Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level | Primary Responsibilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **General Counsel** | Master Access | Global template approval, legal strategy, executive reporting. |
| **Legal Admin** | Elevated Access | Managing playbooks, overriding workflows, high-security vault access. |
| **Procurement/Sales**| Operational | Requesting contracts, initiating negotiation, tracking execution. |
| **Counter-party** | Restricted Access| Reviewing assigned documents, submitting redlines, signing. |
| **Department Head** | Approval Only | Reviewing final contract terms and providing business approval. |
| **Auditor / Finance** | Read-Only | Reviewing contract values and termination dates for reporting. |
