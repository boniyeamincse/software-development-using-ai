# 03 - HRMS System Modules

## Core HR & Records
The foundational directory for employee data, organizational structure, and document storage.

## Talent Acquisition (ATS)
The module focused on external recruitment, from job requisition to the final hire.

## Time & Attendance Engine
The logic center that processes clock-ins, leave requests, and overtime calculations.

## Performance & Rewards Hub
The workspace for performance reviews, goal setting, and compensation adjustment logic.

## Payroll & Finance Sync
The secure module that handles salary disbursement, tax filing, and integration with the ERP/Accounting solution.

## Employee Self-Service (ESS) Portal
The end-user interface where employees manage their own data, requests, and benefits.
