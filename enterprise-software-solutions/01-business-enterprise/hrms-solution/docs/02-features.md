# 02 - HRMS Core Features

## 1. Employee Data Management
- Digital employee profiles with background checks and educational history.
- Dynamic organizational charts with automated reporting line updates.
- Secure document vault for contracts, IDs, and tax forms.

## 2. Recruitment & ATS (Applicant Tracking)
- Job posting distribution to multiple boards and careers page.
- AI-assisted resume parsing and candidate ranking.
- Integrated interview scheduling and feedback collection.

## 3. Time, Attendance & Leave
- Biometric and web-based clock-in/out integration.
- Customizable leave policies (Vacation, Sick, Maternity) with automated accruals.
- Shift planning and roster management with overlap alerts.

## 4. Performance & Talent Development
- 360-degree feedback cycles and annual review automation.
- OKR (Objectives and Key Results) and KPI tracking for individuals and teams.
- Learning Management System (LMS) integration for employee upskilling.

## 5. Payroll & Benefits Administration
- Automated payroll calculation based on attendance, tax, and deductions.
- Multiple currency support and direct bank integration.
- Benefits enrollment portal (Health insurance, 401k/Provident fund).

## 6. Strategic HR Analytics
- Predictive turnover analysis and employee satisfaction heatmaps.
- Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) metrics.
- Resource cost analysis for budget planning.
