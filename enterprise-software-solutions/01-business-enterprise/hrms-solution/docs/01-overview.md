# 01 - HRMS Overview

## Introduction
The **Human Resource Management System (HRMS)** is a comprehensive solution designed to manage the entire employee lifecycle, from recruitment and onboarding to performance management and final offboarding. It serves as the hub for all employee-centric data and workflows within an organization.

## The People Challenge
Managing a growing workforce presents several operational difficulties:
- **Administrative Overhead**: Significant time spent on manual record-keeping and payroll processing.
- **Talent Attrition**: Difficulty in identifying and retaining top talent due to a lack of structured performance tracking.
- **Compliance Gaps**: Risks associated with improper storage of sensitive employee documents and labor law non-compliance.
- **Fragmented Communication**: Barriers between management and staff regarding benefits, leave policies, and company updates.

## The Solution
A secure, cloud-native HR ecosystem that automates core administrative tasks while empowering employees through self-service portals. It features strategic talent management tools, automated payroll integration, and robust reporting to help HR leaders build a more engaged and compliant workforce.

## Core Objectives
- **Centralized Employee Records**: A single, secure database for all professional and personal employee data.
- **Lifecycle Automation**: Streamlined workflows for hiring, onboarding, promotion, and exit.
- **Employee Empowerment**: High-utility self-service tools for leave management, benefit enrollment, and payslip access.
- **Compliance & Privacy**: Ensuring data protection (GDPR/SOC2) and automated adherence to local labor regulations.
