# 13 - HRMS Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI Talent Matching**: Analyzing candidate resumes against the company's most successful historical hires to predict fit.
- **Predictive Turnover AI**: Identifying "Flight Risers" based on engagement patterns and manager feedback.
- **Personalized Learning Paths**: Automated course recommendations for employees based on their performance gap analysis.

## Workforce Modernization
- **Web3 Payments**: Support for salary disbursement in stablecoins for international contractors.
- **Mental Health & Wellness SDK**: Integration with wellness apps to provide proactive stress-alerts and support.

## Ecosystem Growth
- **Universal Employee Identity**: A portable professional ID that carries verified certifications and performance scores.
- **Automated Tax Filing Logic**: Direct integration with government tax portals for real-time withholding and reporting.
