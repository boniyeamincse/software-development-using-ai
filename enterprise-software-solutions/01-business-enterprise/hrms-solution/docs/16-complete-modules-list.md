# 16 - HRMS Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Employee Life-Cycle Hub
- **Universal Profile Master**: Single source of truth for all employee data and history.
- **Document Integrity Vault**: Secure, encrypted storage for contracts and private records.
- **Onboarding/Offboarding orchestrator**: managing tasks and provisioning across departments.
- **Org-Chart & Directory**: real-time visualization of company structure and reporting lines.

## 2. Time, Space & Engagement
- **Global Leave Engine**: handling diverse leave types and complex accrual rules.
- **Dynamic Attendance Hub**: mobile-first, geofenced time tracking and verification.
- **Shift & Roster Planner**: strategic planning for teams and coverage requirements.
- **Employee pulse Survey**: automated tools for measuring sentiment and engagement.

## 3. compensation & Finance
- **Multi-National Payroll Engine**: high-precision calculation of pay, taxes, and benefits.
- **Expense & Reimbursement Tracker**: managing employee claims with automated approval.
- **Bonus & Commission Bridge**: linking performance results to financial payouts.
- **Secure Paystub Gateway**: mobile-accessible record of all historical payments.

## 4. Performance & Excellence
- **360-Degree Review Suite**: holistics feedback tools with AI-driven summaries.
- **Goal & OKR Tracker**: aligning individual tasks with company high-level objectives.
- **Skills Matrix Hub**: identifying and tracking technical and soft skills across the org.
- **Learning & LMS Gateway**: managing internal training and certification programs.

## 5. Operations & Intelligence
- **Talent Acquisition (ATS) Bridge**: (Optional) managing the hiring process and job boards.
- **Workforce Analytics Dashboard**: deep insights into turnover, diversity, and costs.
- **Internal Mobility Engine**: AI-driven recommendations for promotions and transfers.
- **Succession Planning Suite**: identifying future leaders for critical roles.

## 6. Security, Compliance & Admin
- **Global Privacy Guard**: ensuring GDPR/CCPA compliance for sensitive employee data.
- **Granular RBAC Controller**: defining exactly who can see what (Manager, HR, Employee).
- **Compliance Audit Vault**: permanent logs of every record change and access event.
- **System Settings & Globalization**: managing multi-regional rules and holiday calendars.
