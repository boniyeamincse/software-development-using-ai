# 04 - HRMS Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level | Primary Responsibilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **HR Director** | Master Access | High-level analytics, policy approval, executive reporting. |
| **HR Manager** | Elevated Access| Managing employee relations, oversight of payroll and reviews. |
| **Recruiter** | Talent Access | Job postings, candidate communication, offer management. |
| **Payroll Specialist** | Financial Access | Salary calculations, tax compliance, benefits disbursement. |
| **Team Manager** | Managerial Access | Approving leave, conducting reviews for their direct reports. |
| **Employee** | Self-Service | Viewing payslips, requesting leave, participating in reviews. |
