# 03 - ERP System Modules

## Financial Core
The fundamental ledger system that processes all transactions and ensures balanced books across the global enterprise.

## Operations & Supply Chain Hub
Manages the physical movement of goods, from procurement of raw materials to warehouse logistics.

## Manufacturing Control Center
The workspace for production managers to define BOMs, schedule shifts, and monitor assembly line health.

## Executive Command Center
A high-level dashboard offering real-time visibility into the organization’s overall health and KPIs.

## System Administration & Security
The central point for managing user access, system configurations, and integration webhooks.
