# 16 - ERP Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Inventory & Supply Chain
- **Master Warehouse Controller**: Global visibility across all physical locations.
- **Stock Integrity Hub**: Serial, batch, and lot tracking with expiration alerts.
- **Demand Forecasting Engine**: automated re-ordering based on historical trends.
- **Global Freight Bridge**: (Optional) integration with international shipping couriers.

## 2. Manufacturing & Production
- **Production Planner**: scheduling machines, labor, and materials.
- **BOM (Bill of Materials) Hub**: managing complex multi-level product recipes.
- **Quality Control Suite**: automated testing and defect tracking workflows.
- **Equipment Maintenance Monitor**: predictive and scheduled service tracker.

## 3. Financial Management
- **Universal General Ledger**: multi-entity, multi-currency accounting core.
- **Procure-to-Pay Hub**: managing vendors, POs, and accounts payable.
- **Order-to-Cash Hub**: managing customers, invoicing, and accounts receivable.
- **Consolidation Controller**: Real-time financial rollup for global holding companies.

## 4. Human Capital Management (HCM)
- **Global Payroll Engine**: handling multi-national tax and pay rules.
- **Recruitment & Talent Hub**: managing the entire employee lifecycle.
- **Performance & Learning Suite**: internal training and review orchestration.
- **Self-Service Workspace**: employee portal for leaves, paystubs, and info.

## 5. Operations & Workflows
- **Enterprise Workflow Engine**: customizing approval chains for CapEx and more.
- **Project & Job Costing**: tracking profitability for large-scale initiatives.
- **Asset Lifecycle Manager**: managing fixed assets from acquisition to disposal.
- **Fleet & Logistics Bridge**: internal fleet tracking and maintenance.

## 6. Intelligence, Compliance & Admin
- **Executive BI Dashboard**: real-time tracking of entire enterprise health.
- **SOX & legal Audit Vault**: permanent, tamper-proof compliance logging.
- **Global RBAC & Privacy Hub**: security scoped down to regional and row level.
- **IoT & Industry 4.0 Connector**: bridging shop-floor data with business logic.
- **Quality Management (QMS)**: [ISO-compliant quality control](17-quality-management.md).
- **Asset Management (EAM)**: [Global lifecycle tracking](18-enterprise-asset-management.md).
