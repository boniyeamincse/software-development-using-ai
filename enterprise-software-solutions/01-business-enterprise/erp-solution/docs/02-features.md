# 02 - ERP Core Features

## 1. Integrated Financial Management
- General Ledger, Accounts Payable (AP), and Accounts Receivable (AR).
- Automated multi-currency reconciliation and tax calculation.
- Fixed asset management and depreciation tracking.

## 2. Supply Chain & Logistics
- Real-time inventory tracking across multiple warehouses.
- Automated Purchase Order (PO) generation based on low-stock triggers.
- Vendor management and shipment tracking.

## 3. Manufacturing & Production (MRP)
- Bill of Materials (BOM) management and multi-level assembly tracking.
- Production scheduling and capacity planning.
- Quality control checkpoints and waste tracking.

## 4. Sales & Order Fulfillment
- Order orchestrator that links sales to inventory and shipping.
- Support for complex pricing models and contract-based discounts.
- Integrated Returns Management (RMA) workflow.

## 5. Human Capital Synergy (Lite)
- Basic employee profiles, attendance, and department mapping.
- Deep integration with the dedicated HRMS solution for payroll and benefits.

## 6. Business Intelligence & Reporting
- Cross-departmental dashboards (e.g., "Profitability by Product Line").
- Automated month-end closing and financial statement generation.
