# 13 - ERP Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Predictive Inventory AI**: Using machine learning to forecast demand spikes based on economic trends and historic cyclic data.
- **Automated Supply Chain Healing**: AI that automatically shifts procurement to secondary vendors if a primary factory shows signs of slowing down.
- **Conversational ERP**: Natural language voice-interface for querying inventory and financial status ("What is our current cash runway?").

## Modern Ecosystems
- **Blockchain Ledgering**: Immutable, cross-organization audits for international supply chain tracking.
- **IoT-Driven Inventory**: Real-time shelf-weighted sensors and camera-based SKU detection in warehouses.

## Global Expansion
- **One-Click Localization**: Rapid deployment of tax and language modules for new regional offices.
- **Sustainability Dashboard**: Tracking the carbon footprint of the entire supply chain and suggest ECO-optimization.
