# 16 - Compliance Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Governance & Policy Core
- **Framework Mapping Master**: aligning policies with GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, and ISO scripts.
- **Policy Lifecycle Suite**: managing the creation, review, and versioning of internal rules.
- **Acceptance & Awareness Hub**: tracking employee training and policy attestations.
- **Regulatory Intelligence Bridge**: monitoring changes in global laws and requirements.

## 2. Evidence & Monitoring
- **Immutable Evidence Vault**: Secure, WORM-compliant storage for audit proof.
- **Continuous Monitoring Hub**: Real-time integration with cloud and local infra.
- **Evidence Discovery Engine**: automated collection of logs and configurations.
- **Control health Monitor**: real-time tracking of control effectiveness.

## 3. Audit & Remediation
- **Audit orchestrator**: managing schedules, tasks, and communications for audits.
- **Remediation Task Hub**: tracking the resolution of identified compliance gaps.
- **Auditor Collaboration Workspace**: Secure area for external auditors to review data.
- **Gap & finding Analytics**: identifying recurring failures and systemic risks.

## 4. Risk & Vendor Management
- **Enterprise Risk Register**: Centralized hub for identifying and scoring business risks.
- **Vendor security Portal**: managing the compliance lifecycle of third-party partners.
- **Automated Scorecard Engine**: Calculating risk and compliance scores for the org.
- **Mitigation Strategy Tracker**: managing the progress of risk-reduction initiatives.

## 5. Privacy & Ethics
- **Subject Rights (DSAR) Gateway**: managing data access and deletion requests.
- **PII Discovery & Inventory**: (Optional) mapping personal data across systems.
- **Ethics & Whistleblower Hub**: Secure, anonymous channel for reporting violations.
- **Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA)**: managing the review of new projects for privacy.

## 6. Intelligence, Trust & Admin
- **Executive GRC Dashboard**: high-level visibility into compliance posture.
- **AI Regulatory Summarizer**: condensing complex legal updates into actions.
- **Global Compliance Audit log**: permanent, tamper-proof record of all activity.
- **Security & RBAC Controller**: managing granular access to sensitive GRC data.
