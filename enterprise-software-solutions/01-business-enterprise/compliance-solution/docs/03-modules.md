# 03 - Compliance System Modules

## Policy Portal
The central repository for creating, versioning, and distributing corporate and regulatory policies.

## Controls & Monitoring Engine
The background logic that periodically scans infrastructure and applications to verify compliant states.

## Risk Assessment Workspace
The tool for identifying, documenting, and scoring operational and regulatory risks.

## Audit & Evidence Vault
The high-security repository where evidence is collected, tagged, and presented to auditors.

## Learning & Awareness Center
Manages employee compliance training, policy attestations, and certification tracking.

## Ethics & Incident Manager
The secure module for reporting, investigating, and remediating compliance or ethical breaches.
