# 02 - Compliance Core Features

## 1. Multi-Framework Mapping
- **Standard Library**: Built-in support for SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and more.
- **Cross-Framework Mapping**: Map a single internal control to multiple external regulations to reduce redundancy.
- **Custom Policy Builder**: Version-controlled document editor for internal handbooks and SLAs.

## 2. Automated Evidence Collection
- **Cloud Integrations**: Automatically fetch security configurations from AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
- **API Documentation Sync**: Ensure API security and data privacy controls are documented and active.
- **Personnel Verification**: Automated tracking of background checks and signed NDA statuses.

## 3. Continuous Monitoring & GRC
- **Real-Time Dashboards**: Visualization of current "Compliance Health" across the enterprise.
- **Automated Alerts**: Immediate notification of "Critical Failures" (e.g., an unencrypted database in production).
- **Risk Scoring**: Quantitative assessment of vulnerabilities based on impact and likelihood.

## 4. Audit Management Hub
- **Audit Workflow**: Dedicated space for external auditors to view requested evidence within scoped permissions.
- **Evidence Gaps**: Visual tracker of missing documentation or failing controls.
- **Immutable Log Submission**: Cryptographically signed evidence packages to ensure data integrity.

## 5. Training & Attestation
- **Mandatory Course Tracking**: Assign and monitor compliance training (e.g., Anti-Bribery, Security Awareness).
- **Digital Sign-Off**: Legally binding attestation for policy acknowledgments.
- **Quiz Engine**: Verified understanding of critical corporate policies.

## 6. Incident & Reporting
- **Whistleblower Hot-line**: Secure, anonymous portal for reporting ethical or compliance breaches.
- **Remediation Tracking**: Workflow for assigning and closing tasks related to compliance failures.
- **Executive Reporting**: High-level summaries for the Board of Directors.
