# 13 - Compliance Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI-Autonomous Auditor**: ML models that can "Read" complex vendor contracts and SOC reports, automatically flags gaps in 3rd-party compliance.
- **Predictive Risk AI**: Identifying patterns of non-compliance (e.g., specific departments failing training) before they lead to a security breach.
- **Reg-as-Code Engine**: Allowing compliance teams to write requirements in a natural-language format that is automatically converted into technical scan rules.

## Ecosystem Expansion
- **Direct Government Integration**: Real-time regulatory feed that automatically updates the internal framework requirement list.
- **Universal Trust Link**: A public-facing (but secure) page for enterprises to share their real-time compliance status with prospective clients.

## Modern Governance
- **DAO Governance Modes**: Support for decentralized policy voting and approval via smart-contracts.
- **ESG Maturity Tracking**: Detailed metrics for Environmental, Social, and Governance compliance for modern ethical investing standards.
