# 03 - Project System Modules

## Project Planning & Roadmap
The strategic module for defining project scopes, milestones, and high-level timelines.

## Task Execution Engine
The daily workspace where tasks are managed, assigned, and updated via Boards or Lists.

## Resource & Capacity Hub
The visualization center for team workloads, skills distribution, and availability.

## Financial & Budgeting Center
The module dedicated to tracking labor costs, project expenses, and overall budget health.

## Document & Asset Vault
The centralized repository for all project-related files, proposals, and design artifacts.

## Portfolio Dashboard (PPM)
The executive-level view of all active and upcoming projects across the organization.
