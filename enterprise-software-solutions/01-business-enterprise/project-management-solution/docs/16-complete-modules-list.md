# 16 - Project Management Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Planning & Orchestration
- **Universal Project Master**: Single console for managing all initiatives and portfolios.
- **Dynamic Gantt Engine**: High-fidelity scheduling with dependency and critical path logic.
- **Strategic Kanban Hub**: specialized boards for Agile, Lean, and custom workflows.
- **Milestone & Sprint Planner**: Organizing work into high-impact delivery cycles.

## 2. Resource & Time Hub
- **Capacity & Workload Balancer**: Real-time visibility into team availability and heatmaps.
- **Precision Time-Tracker**: Granular tracking linked to specific tasks and projects.
- **Timesheet Approval Orchestrator**: workflow-driven validation of work hours for billing.
- **Skill-based Allocator**: Matching the right team members to the right projects.

## 3. Collaboration & Workspace
- **Collaborative Document Suite**: Real-time co-editing for specs and internal docs.
- **Omni-channel Discussion Hub**: Contextual threads, @mentions, and real-time alerts.
- **Enterprise File Vault**: Secure, version-controlled storage for all project assets.
- **Meeting & Workshop Bridge**: (Optional) integration with Zoom/Teams for sync work.

## 4. Financial & Billing Sync
- **Project Budget Controller**: tracking costs (Labor & Expenses) against projections.
- **Automated Billing Bridge**: Converting approved hours and milestones into invoices.
- **Procurement & Vendor Link**: managing third-party costs related to projects.
- **ROI & margin Tracker**: analyzing profitability at the project and portfolio level.

## 5. Intelligence & Visibility
- **Portfolio Health Dashboard (PPM)**: High-level visibility for executives and PMOs.
- **AI Delay & Risk Shield**: Predictive models identifying milestones at risk.
- **Velocity & Trend Analytics**: analyzing team performance and throughput over time.
- **Custom Report Architect**: Powerful tools for creating tailored stakeholder reports.

## 6. Security, Governance & Admin
- **Immutable Project Audit Vault**: Tamper-proof logging of every project change.
- **Multi-tenant RBAC Hub**: separating internal work from guest/client access.
- **Compliance & Policy Engine**: Enforcing checklists and standards before stage-moves.
- **Internationalization & Calendar Hub**: managing diverse timezones and holidays.
