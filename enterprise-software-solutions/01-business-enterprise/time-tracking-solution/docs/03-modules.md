# 03 - Time System Modules

## Time Entry Core
The primary interface for users to log hours, featuring timers, manual entry forms, and calendar views.

## Approval & Verification Hub
The workspace for managers to review, audit, and approve submitted timesheets.

## Rate & Billing Engine
The module that manages billable rates, cost rates, and transforms hours into financial data.

## Policy & Compliance Manager
The engine room for defining labor rules, overtime triggers, and mandatory break requirements.

## Analytics & Reporting Suite
The visualization layer for analyzing enterprise-wide productivity and project profitability.

## Integration Bridge
Coordinates the bi-directional data flow between Time Tracking, Project Management, and Payroll/Accounting.
