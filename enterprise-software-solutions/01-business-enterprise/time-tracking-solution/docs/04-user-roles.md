# 04 - Time Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level | Primary Responsibilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Operations Director** | Master Access | Global policy setting, enterprise productivity reporting. |
| **Project Manager** | Elevated Access| Approving team time, monitoring project burn, billing review. |
| **Team Member** | Operational | Logging hours, managing personal timers, submitting timesheets. |
| **Payroll Admin** | Financial Access | Extracting approved hours for wage calculation, audit review. |
| **Client (External)** | Portal Access | Viewing billable logs and summary reports for their projects. |
| **Auditor** | Read-Only | Periodic review of time log history for compliance. |
