# 02 - POS Core Features

## 1. High-Velocity Checkout
- **Touch-Optimized UI**: Customizable quick-action grids for popular items.
- **Barcode & QR Scanning**: Rapid ingestion of SKUs and digital coupons.
- **Elastic Search**: Sub-second product lookup for items without tags.

## 2. Integrated Payment Orchestration
- **Universal Payment Support**: Credit/Debit (EMV), Apple/Google Pay (NFC).
- **Split Payments**: Easily divide totals between multiple methods or people.
- **Digital & Thermal Receipts**: SMS/Email options alongside high-speed printing.

## 3. Real-Time Inventory & Catalog
- **Live Stock Sync**: Dynamic updates to the central ERP as items are sold.
- **Multi-Location Visibility**: Check stock availability in nearby branches instantly.
- **Automated Reordering**: Low-stock alerts triggered at the register.

## 4. Customer Engagement & Loyalty
- **Profile Lookup**: Recognize customers via phone, email, or physical card.
- **Dynamic Rewards**: Live point accrual and transparent redemption at checkout.
- **Personalized Offers**: Targeted discounts based on historical purchase data.

## 5. Offline Resiliency
- **Local Cache Mode**: Continue processing sales during internet outages.
- **Background Sync**: Automatic reconciliation of offline data once connection restores.
- **Offline Payment Queuing**: Securely store transaction tokens for later processing.

## 6. Managerial Controls
- **Flexible Pricing**: On-the-fly discounts, markdowns, and price-overrides.
- **Shift Management**: Cash drawer reconciliation and "Blind Drops."
- **Staff Tracking**: Detailed sales-per-employee reporting.
