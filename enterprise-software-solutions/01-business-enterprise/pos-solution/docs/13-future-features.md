# 13 - POS Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Computer Vision Checkout**: Automatic item identification via cameras, eliminating the need for barcode scanning.
- **Predictive Up-selling AI**: Real-time suggestions for cashiers based on current basket contents and customer history.
- **Queue Management AI**: Predicting peak traffic times and alerting managers to open new registers proactively.

## Modern Experiences
- **Self-Checkout Kiosk Mode**: Transforming any tablet into a customer-facing kiosk with a single toggle.
- **Augmented Reality (AR) Inventory**: Using mobile devices to "See through" shelves to identify low stock items via AR overlays.
- **Biometric Payments**: Allowing loyalty members to pay and check out using "Smile-to-Pay" facial recognition.

## Global Expansion
- **Universal Tax Engine**: Real-time, geo-aware tax calculations for every sovereign territory globally.
- **Local Payment Method SDKs**: Rapid integration with hundreds of local payment wallets worldwide.
