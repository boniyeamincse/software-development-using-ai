# 03 - POS System Modules

## Transactional Register
The primary touch-interface for ring-ups, payment processing, and receipt generation.

## Catalog & Menu Manager
The backend module for organizing products into categories, modifiers, and price tiers.

## Loyalty & CRM Link
Handles customer identification, point balances, and coupon validation logic.

## Physical Asset Integrator
Manages drivers and connectivity for printers, scanners, scales, and payment terminals.

## Inventory Sync Service
The background worker that communicates sales data to the central ERP/Warehouse.

## Store Manager Portal
The administrative layer for closing shifts, analyzing local sales, and managing staff permissions.
