# 16 - POS Solution Complete Modules List

## 1. Checkout & Transaction Hub
- **Fiscal Transaction Core**: handling high-frequency sales with ACID properties.
- **Split-Payment Orchestrator**: managing complex tender combinations (Cash, Card, Gift).
- **Promotion & Discount Engine**: Enforcing store rules and conditional pricing.
- **precision Receipt Generator**: creating ESC/POS and digital receipts with fiscal IDs.

## 2. Retail Interface & UX
- **Touch-Speed Frontend**: specialized React interface for fast-paced retail.
- **Barcode & Scanner Bridge**: managing interaction with HID and USB devices.
- **Quick-Service Catalog**: visual menus for non-barcoded or frequent items.
- **Off-line Resilience Master**: ensuring sales continue without active internet.

## 3. Inventory & Supply Chain Sync
- **multi-Location Stock Bridge**: Real-time decrementing of inventory across stores.
- **Central ERP Connector**: syncing sales data with the enterprise core.
- **Stock replenishment Trigger**: automated alerts for low inventory at the terminal.
- **Price-book Sync Hub**: managing global price updates and localized taxes.

## 4. Loyalty & Growth Suite
- **Omni-channel Loyalty Hub**: tracking points and rewards across store and web.
- **Gift Card & Credit Vault**: managing digital and physical value-records.
- **Customer Identity Panel**: providing cashiers with history and preference data.
- **Marketing & coupon Bridge**: validating and attributing retail-specific promos.

## 5. Operations & Intelligence
- **terminal Health Monitor**: tracking hardware status and security across devices.
- **Store-KPI Dashboard**: real-time tracking of ATV, Volume, and margins.
- **Shift & Drawer Manager**: handling register openings, closings, and reconciliations.
- **Employee performance Hub**: tracking sales metrics and incentives per staff member.

## 6. Security, Governance & Admin
- **Immutable Fiscal Audit Vault**: Tamper-proof logging for legal and tax compliance.
- **AI Upsell & Next-Action AI**: recommending additions and attachments at checkout.
- **Remote Lockdown Hub**: security controls for loss prevention and fraud.
- **Fiscal Law Policy Engine**: managing region-specific tax and numbering rules.
