# 03 - Feedback & Review Modules

## Management Dashboard (Admin)
The primary workspace for brand managers to monitor ratings, respond to reviews, and configure automation rules.

## Review Submission Portal
A lightweight, mobile-optimized interface where customers rate their experience and upload photos.

## Social Proof Widgets
A collection of embeddable UI components (Carousels, Lists, Wall of Love) for marketing websites.

## NLP & Sentiment Engine
A backend microservice that processes raw text to determine mood and extract topics using Natural Language Processing.

## Integration Hub
Connects with external platforms (Google, Facebook) and internal systems (Loyalty, CDP) for data synchronization.
