# 02 - Feedback Core Features

## 1. Automated Review Requests
- Multi-channel solicitation via Email, SMS, and WhatsApp after a purchase or service.
- Smart timing: Sending requests based on delivery status or service completion.

## 2. Omnichannel Review Management
- Aggregating reviews from Google My Business, Facebook, Yelp, and Internal forms into one inbox.
- Unified response management: Reply to all platforms from a single interface.

## 3. SEO-Optimized Review Widgets
- Embeddable trust badges and review carousels for e-commerce product pages.
- Schema.org "AggregateRating" markup for Google Search rich snippets.

## 4. AI Sentiment Analysis
- Automated tagging of reviews: Positive, Neutral, Negative.
- Topic extraction: Identifying recurring keywords (e.g., "Slow Shipping," "Great Quality").

## 5. Dispute & Moderation Workflow
- Tools to flag fraudulent or abusive reviews based on community guidelines.
- Internal "Early Warning System" for negative sentiment spikes.

## 6. Incentivized Feedback
- Integration with Loyalty & Rewards systems to offer points for verified reviews.
- "Verified Buyer" badges to combat fake feedback.
