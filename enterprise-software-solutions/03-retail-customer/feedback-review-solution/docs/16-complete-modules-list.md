# 16 - Feedback & Review Complete Modules List

## 1. Feedback Acquisition
- **Intelligent Capture Widget**: Multi-attribute rating and text entry client.
- **Rich Media Uploader**: Secure handling for customer photos and videos.
- **Dynamic Survey Builder**: creating branching questionnaires for tiered feedback.
- **Invitations & Reminders**: Automated follow-ups post-purchase or service.

## 2. Trust & Integrity
- **Verification Engine**: Linking feedback to genuine transaction data.
- **Spam & Fraud Guard**: protecting the platform from bots and manipulation.
- **Review Policy Enforcement**: automated flagging of prohibited content.
- **Identity & Privacy Proxy**: protecting user data while displaying public reviews.

## 3. Intelligence & Processing
- **Deep Sentiment Analysis**: Extracting emotional tone from raw text.
- **Topic & Keyword Clustering**: Automatically identifying common themes.
- **Language Translation Layer**: enabling global feedback reading for admins.
- **Trend Discovery Engine**: alerting admins to sudden shifts in sentiment.

## 4. Moderation & Operations
- **Administrator Review Portal**: Central queue for approving or rejecting content.
- **Closed-Loop Alert System**: notifying support teams of critical negative feedback.
- **Appeal & Resolution Workflow**: handling cases where customers want to change reviews.
- **Comment & Interaction Hub**: allowing brands to reply publicly to customers.

## 5. Marketing & Social Proof
- **UGC Gallery Module**: Displaying curated customer media on product pages.
- **SEO Rich Snippets Bridge**: pushing rating data to search engine results.
- **Social Proof Notifications**: "Verified buyer recently rated this 5 stars."
- **Incentive & Badge Manager**: awarding points or coupons for valuable feedback.

## 6. Business Intelligence (BI)
- **NPS & CSAT Dashboard**: Tracking standard experience metrics.
- **Competitor Sentiment Comparison**: (Optional) analyzing market-wide feedback.
- **Executive Performance Digest**: summarizing brand health for leadership.
- **Voice of the Customer (VoC) Reports**: transforming feedback into product roadmap tasks.
