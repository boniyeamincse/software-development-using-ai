# 13 - Feedback Future Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Video Review Analysis**: AI that analyzes the facial expressions and vocal tone of video reviews.
- **Automatic Review Summarizer**: Generating a 1-sentence summary for thousands of reviews (e.g., "Most customers loved the durability but found it slightly heavy").

## Integrated Experiences
- **AR Interaction**: Leaving geo-synced "AR Reviews" at physical store locations.
- **NFT Proof-of-Purchase**: Using blockchain-based receipts to grant non-transferable voting rights on future product designs.

## Marketplace & Social
- **Shoppable Reviews**: Turning customer photos directly into buyable product links within the widget.
- **Social Media Auto-Poster**: AI that identifies high-quality reviews and automatically crops them into Instagram Stories or TikTok templates.
