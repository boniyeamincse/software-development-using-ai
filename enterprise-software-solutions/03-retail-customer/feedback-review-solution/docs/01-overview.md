# 01 - Feedback & Review Overview

## Introduction
The **Feedback & Review Solution** is a powerful reputation management platform designed for retail, e-commerce, and service-based businesses. It enables organizations to collect, manage, and showcase customer sentiment across multiple digital touchpoints.

## The Reputation Challenge
Online reputation is a critical driver of sales, yet businesses face:
- **Low Review Volume**: Customers often forget to leave feedback unless prompted.
- **Fragmented Feedback**: Reviews scattered across Google, Yelp, and internal portals.
- **Unmanaged Sentiment**: Negative reviews left unaddressed, damaging brand trust.
- **Lack of Actionable Insights**: Difficulty in identifying recurring product issues from raw text feedback.

## The Solution
A centralized feedback hub that automates review solicitation via post-purchase emails and SMS. It features AI-driven sentiment analysis, unified multi-platform management, and SEO-optimized review widgets to boost conversion rates.

## Core Objectives
- **Automated Collection**: Intelligent triggers that ask for reviews at the peak of customer satisfaction.
- **Unified Management**: A "Single Pane of Glass" for responding to reviews across Google, Facebook, and Internal sites.
- **Social Proof Integration**: Dynamic widgets that showcase top reviews on the website to drive trust.
- **Sentiment Intelligence**: AI tools that distill thousands of reviews into actionable product improvements.
