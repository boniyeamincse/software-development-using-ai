# 11 - Feedback Security & Moderation

## Data Trust
- **Verified Reviews**: Integration with Order Management to verify that the reviewer actually purchased the item.
- **Anti-Spam**: Google reCAPTCHA v3 on all submission portals.

## Content Safety
- **AI Profanity Shield**: Multi-language filtering for hate speech and inappropriate content.
- **PII Protection**: Automated scrubbing of names/addresses from public-facing review text.

## Privacy
- **GDPR compliance**: Providing customers the ability to delete their review history.
- **Encryption**: AES-256 at rest for all stored sensitive data.
