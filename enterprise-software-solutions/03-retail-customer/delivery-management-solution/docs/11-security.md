# 11 - Delivery Security & Privacy

## Location Privacy
- **Geographic Blurring**: Only allowing relevant parties to see precise driver coordinates.
- **Data Retention**: Automatically purging precise GPS history after 90 days for privacy compliance.

## System Integrity
- **Device Authentication**: Ensuring ONLY authorized fleet devices can push telemetry or update delivery status.
- **Tamper-Proof ePOD**: Digital signing of proof-of-delivery files to ensure they weren't altered after capture.

## Compliance
- **GDPR**: Tools for managing recipient address data deletion.
- **E-Signature Laws**: Ensuring digital signatures meet local legal standards (e.g., eIDAS).
