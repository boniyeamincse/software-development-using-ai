# 02 - Delivery Core Features

## 1. Automated Route Optimization
- Support for multi-stop routing with constraints (Time windows, Vehicle capacity).
- Integration with Google Maps and Mapbox for real-time traffic-aware routing.

## 2. Real-Time Fleet Tracking
- Live GPS tracking of all active drivers on a centralized map.
- "Geofencing" alerts when a driver enters or leaves a specific delivery zone.

## 3. Driver Mobile App
- Task management: View assigned stops, navigate, and update status.
- Electronic Proof of Delivery (ePOD): Capture pictures, signatures, and barcodes.

## 4. Customer "Track & Trace" Portal
- White-labeled tracking link for customers with live driver location.
- Automatic ETA updates and "Driver is Arriving" push notifications.

## 5. Automated Dispatch Engine
- Auto-assign orders based on driver proximity, historical performance, or current load.
- Manual "Drag-and-Drop" dispatching for exception handling.

## 6. Returns & Reverse Logistics
- Managed workflows for picking up items from customers and returning them to the warehouse.
- Intelligent consolidation of delivery and return trips.
