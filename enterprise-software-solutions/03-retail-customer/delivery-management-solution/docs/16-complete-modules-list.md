# 16 - Delivery Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Fleet & Asset Management
- **Vehicle Profile Hub**: Tracking dimensions, capacity, and maintenance schedules.
- **Driver Identity & Docs**: managing licenses, ratings, and certifications.
- **Fuel & Expense Tracker**: Monitoring operational costs per vehicle.
- **Resource Allocator**: Assigning drivers to specific vehicles and shifts.

## 2. Dynamic Routing & Dispatch
- **Master Route Optimizer**: Automated, traffic-aware delivery path planning.
- **Real-time Dispatcher**: matching urgent pickups with the nearest driver.
- **Service area Manager**: Geo-fencing and territory definitions.
- **Dynamic Slot Windows**: managing customer-requested delivery times.

## 3. Live Logistics Operations
- **Track-and-Trace Gateway**: WebSocket-driven real-time vehicle mapping.
- **ETA Intelligence Service**: proactive time estimation based on live data.
- **Proof of Delivery (PoD) Suite**: Capturing signatures, photos, and GPS data.
- **Barcode & QR Scanner**: ensuring the right package is at the right door.

## 4. Driver Companion Experience
- **Mobile Workflow Client**: Complete task management for field staff.
- **Offline Integrity Layer**: handling data capture in low-connectivity areas.
- **Navigation Bridge**: Deep-linking to Google Maps/Waze/here with optimized paths.
- **Driver Earnings & Stats**: Real-time feedback on daily performance.

## 5. Communication & Trust
- **Customer delivery Portal**: branded view for users to watch their package move.
- **Privacy Proxy (Masking)**: secure voice/text between driver and customer.
- **Geofence Notification Hub**: Automated alerts based on proximity.
- **Dispute Resolution Workspace**: investigating missing or damaged items.

## 6. Intelligence & Compliance
- **Logistics BI Dashboard**: analyzing cost-per-mile and route efficiency.
- **Safety & Behavior Analytics**: Monitoring driver habits and safety metrics.
- **Package Lifecycle Audit**: Permanent, high-fidelity log of every handoff.
- **Volume Forecasting AI**: Predicting future demand for resource planning.
