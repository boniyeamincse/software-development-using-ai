# 13 - Delivery Roadmap

## Next-Gen Logistics
- **Autonomous Fleet Integration**: Support for dispatching to sidewalk robots and delivery drones.
- **IoT Smart Lock Integration**: Allowing drivers to securely drop packages inside a garage or smart-locker via API.

## Advanced AI
- **Predictive Arrival Time**: ML models that factor in historic weather, parking difficulty, and driver habits.
- **Automatic Damage Detection**: Using AI on the driver's phone to identify package damage via the camera.

## Financial & Eco
- **Driver Payout Engine**: Automated per-delivery payout calculations including tips and fuel surcharges.
- **Carbon Offset Tracker**: Reporting on the environmental impact of delivery routes and suggesting EV alternatives.
