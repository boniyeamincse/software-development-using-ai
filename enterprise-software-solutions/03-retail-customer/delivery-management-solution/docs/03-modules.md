# 03 - Delivery System Modules

## Operations Control Center (Dispatcher)
The command center for logistics managers to monitor the fleet, manage orders, and handle real-time delivery exceptions.

## Driver Mobile Application
A native mobile app for drivers to receive tasks, navigate optimized routes, and capture proof of delivery.

## Customer Tracking Widget
A lightweight, embeddable component that gives customers a real-time view of their delivery progress.

## Route Planning Engine
A backend service that handles the heavy computation of optimizing hundreds of stops across dozens of vehicles.

## Analytics & Fleet Reports
Generates deep insights into "On-Time Performance," "Cost per Delivery," and "Fuel Efficiency."
