# 16 - Customer Support Complete Modules List

## 1. Ticketing & Routing Core
- **Omni-channel Aggregator**: Centralizing requests from Web, Email, and Social.
- **Skill-Based Router**: Matching tickets to the best-qualified agents.
- **Priority Scoring Engine**: automated triage based on SLA and sentiment.
- **Internal Notes & Collaboration**: private workspace for agent teamwork.

## 2. Live Engagement
- **Real-time Chat Engine**: High-performance, low-latency customer messaging.
- **Agent Peeking & Ghosting**: Specialized tools for active session monitoring.
- **Secure File Exchange**: Safe handling of screenshots and diagnostic files.
- **Presence & Availability Hub**: Tracking agent status (Online, Away, Lunch).

## 3. Knowledge & Self-Service
- **Public Help Center**: Searchable repository of articles and FAQs.
- **Internal KB for Agents**: specialized documentation for technical support.
- **AI Suggestion Bot**: surfacing relevant articles during chat.
- **Community Forum Module**: peer-to-peer support and discussion.

## 4. AI & Automation
- **Smart Reply Assistant**: AI-generated draft responses for agents.
- **Automatic Ticket Clustering**: identifying emerging trends and issues.
- **Sentiment & Emotional Intelligence**: Real-time analysis of customer tone.
- **Macro & Workflow Automator**: Triggering bulk actions based on rules.

## 5. Performance & SLA
- **SLA Breach Monitor**: proactive tracking of response and resolution goals.
- **Agent Productivity Suite**: metrics on handle time, resolution rate, and load.
- **Executive BI Dashboard**: High-level views of support ROI and health.
- **CSAT/NPS Survey Engine**: automated feedback loop with customers.

## 6. Trust, Privacy & Admin
- **PII Redaction Service**: automated stripping of sensitive data from logs.
- **Compliance & Audit Vault**: Permanent record of all customer interactions.
- **GDPR/CCPA Privacy Suite**: handling data access and deletion requests.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: granular permissions for support staff.
