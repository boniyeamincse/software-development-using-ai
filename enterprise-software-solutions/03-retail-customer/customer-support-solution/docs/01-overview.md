# 01 - Customer Support Overview

## Introduction
The **Customer Support Solution** is an omnichannel support platform designed for retail and service-oriented businesses. it centralizes customer inquiries from web, email, and social channels into a single, unified workspace for support agents.

## The Support Challenge
Modern businesses struggle with:
- **Fragmented Channels**: Inquiries scattered across email, WhatsApp, and web forms.
- **Slow Response Times**: Manual triaging leading to delayed resolutions.
- **Lack of Context**: Agents not knowing the customer's purchase history or past interactions.
- **Inefficient Self-Service**: Customers having to contact support for basic information (e.g., order status).

## The Solution
An intelligent, AI-powered helpdesk that features automated ticketing, real-time internal collaboration, and a comprehensive self-service knowledge base. It provides a 360-degree view of the customer by integrating with the order management system.

## Core Objectives
- **Omnichannel Consolidation**: A single "Universal Inbox" for all customer touchpoints.
- **Resolution Speed**: Automated SLA tracking and intelligent ticket routing.
- **Agent Productivity**: Canned responses, internal notes, and AI-suggested solutions.
- **Customer Empowerment**: A robust self-service portal to deflect common queries.
