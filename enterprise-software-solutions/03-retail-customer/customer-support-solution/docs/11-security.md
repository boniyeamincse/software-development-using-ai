# 11 - Customer Support Security

## Data Privacy
- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 for traffic; AES-256 for ticket history at rest.
- **GDPR Readiness**: Ability to "Right to be Forgotten" - wiping customer history across all support channels.

## Operational Safety
- **RBAC**: Strict role-based access control preventing junior agents from seeing high-level billing data.
- **Audit Trails**: Detailed log showing who viewed, edited, or responded to a ticket and when.

## Compliance
- **SOC2 Type II**: Regular audits of internal data handling procedures.
- **DLP (Data Loss Prevention)**: Automatic masking of credit card numbers or sensitive IDs in ticket descriptions.
