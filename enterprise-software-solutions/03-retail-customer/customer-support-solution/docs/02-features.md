# 02 - Customer Support Features

## 1. Omnichannel Ticketing
- Unified inbox for Email, Web Chat, WhatsApp, and Facebook Messenger.
- Automated ticket creation from incoming communications.

## 2. Intelligent Routing & Triaging
- Skill-based routing to ensure tickets reach the right department (e.g., Billing, Technical).
- Keyword-based automated tagging and prioritization.

## 3. Knowledge Base & Self-Service
- Public-facing Help Center with searchable FAQs and tutorials.
- Internal wiki for agent training and standard operating procedures (SOPs).

## 4. Real-Time Collaboration
- Internal notes and "@mentions" within tickets for cross-team collaboration.
- Collision detection to prevent multiple agents from replying to the same ticket.

## 5. SLA & Performance Management
- Customizable Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with automated escalation rules.
- Real-time dashboards showing "Time to First Response" and "Average Resolution Time."

## 6. AI-Powered Support (Copilot)
- Automated ticket summarization for quick agent catch-up.
- Suggestion engine for canned responses and knowledge base articles.
