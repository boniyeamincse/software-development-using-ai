# 13 - Customer Support Roadmap

## Autonomous Support
- **Full AI-Agent Integration**: Chatbots that can resolve 60% of common queries without human intervention by performing actions (e.g., triggering a refund).
- **Proactive Ticket Creation**: Detecting customer frustration patterns on the website and preemptively opening a support session.

## Advanced Voice Support
- **AI Interactive Voice Response (IVR)**: Natural language phone menus.
- **Real-time Sentiment Analysis**: Pinging managers if a live call's sentiment turns highly negative.

## Integrated Marketplaces
- **Support-to-Sales Bridge**: Allowing agents to suggest related products directly within the support chat based on past purchase history.
