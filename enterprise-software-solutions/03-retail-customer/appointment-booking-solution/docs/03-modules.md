# 03 - Appointment Booking Modules

## Customer Booking Front-End
The public-facing portal or widget where customers select services, staff, and time slots. Optimized for mobile and desktop.

## Admin Dashboard & Operations
The central control panel for managers to view daily calendars, manage staff rosters, and configure service pricing.

## Staff Personal Portal
A dedicated view for service providers to see their upcoming schedule, manage their availability, and update client notes.

## Notification & Reminder Engine
An automated service that orchestrates all outbound communications based on trigger points (Booking, 24h Reminder, Follow-up).

## Analytics & Utilization Reporter
Generates reports on "Peak Booking Hours," "Staff Performance," and "No-Show Trends" to help business owners optimize operations.
