# 02 - Appointment Booking Features

## 1. Multi-Resource Scheduling
- Book multiple resources (Staff + Room + Equipment) in a single transaction.
- Dynamic slot calculation based on the combination of available resources.

## 2. Real-Time Availability Sync
- Instant updates across web, mobile, and internal admin panels.
- Two-way sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal.

## 3. Automated Notifications
- Customizable SMS, Email, and Push notifications for confirmations and reminders.
- "Last-minute opening" alerts for waitlisted customers.

## 4. Deposits & Integrated Payments
- Securely collect booking fees or full payments using global gateways.
- Support for "No-Show" penalty charges and automated refund logic.

## 5. Client Self-Service Portal
- Simple interface for customers to reschedule or cancel based on business policy.
- Access to past appointment history and upcoming bookings.

## 6. Staff Management & Rostering
- Define working hours, breaks, and vacations for each staff member.
- Skills-based routing to ensure the right staff is booked for the right service.
