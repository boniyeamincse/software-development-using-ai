# 13 - Appointment Future Roadmap

## AI-Driven Optimization
- **Predictive Staffing**: AI modeling that suggests shift changes based on historic booking peaks.
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Adjusting service costs during high-demand or low-demand slots.

## Integrated Experiences
- **Video Consultation Integration**: One-click Zoom/Teams link generation for virtual appointments.
- **In-App Wallet**: A loyalty-based wallet for pre-paying and managing gift cards.

## Voice Recognition
- **AI Appointment Chatbots**: Allowing customers to book services via phone/voice assistants (Alexa/Google Home).
