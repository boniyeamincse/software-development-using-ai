# 16 - Appointment Booking Complete Modules List

## 1. Scheduling Core
- **Universal Availability Engine**: managing complex slots across providers and locations.
- **Resource Allocation Manager**: Linking rooms and equipment to specific bookings.
- **Timezone Orchestrator**: Ensuring global accuracy for providers and customers.
- **Buffer & Duration Controller**: Handling set-up/tear-down times automatically.

## 2. Customer Booking Experience
- **Adaptive Multi-Step Widget**: Specialized UX for fast, friction-free booking.
- **Customer Intake Portal**: Secure collection of necessary info before arrival.
- **Intelligent Waitlist**: automated notification for newly available high-demand slots.
- **Group & Class Manager**: Handling multi-seat sessions and workshops.

## 3. Communication & engagement
- **Multi-Channel Alert Gateway**: Push, SMS, and Email reminders.
- **Two-Way SMS Hub**: allowing customers to manage bookings via text.
- **Confirmation & Reschedule Logic**: automated handling of status changes.
- **Automated Review Invites**: Driving trust through post-visit feedback.

## 4. Financial Operations
- **Deposit & Pre-payment Hub**: Integrated Stripe/PayPal for secure funds.
- **No-Show Protection Engine**: Managing card holds and late-cancellation fees.
- **Invoice & Receipt Generator**: automated professional billing post-service.
- **Sales & Commission Tracker**: splitting revenue for independent providers.

## 5. Staff & Admin Workspace
- **Master Calendar Dashboard**: Real-time view of all appointments and resources.
- **Provider Management Portal**: Staff handling their own shifts and breaks.
- **Customer Directory (CRM)**: Historical booking data and preferences.
- **Shift & Roster Builder**: Strategic planning for optimal coverage.

## 6. Intelligence & Integration
- **Executive Utilization Dashboard**: Analytics on staffing and revenue ROI.
- **Calendar Sync Bridge**: Real-time 2-way sync with Google/Outlook/iCal.
- **Virtual Session Gateway**: Auto-generating Zoom/Teams links for online bookings.
- **AI Schedule Optimizer**: Predicting peak times and suggesting shift changes.
