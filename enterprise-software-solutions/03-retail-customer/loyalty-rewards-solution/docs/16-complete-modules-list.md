# 16 - Loyalty & Rewards Complete Modules List

## 1. Membership & Ledger
- **High-Precision Points Ledger**: Transactional record of all point movements.
- **Points Expiration Manager**: Handling rolling and fixed-date expirations.
- **Profile & Tier Tracker**: managing user levels and progression history.
- **Multi-Account Linking**: allowing family members to pool points (optional).

## 2. Earnings & rules
- **Dynamic Rule Engine**: executing complex logic for point distribution.
- **Event Listener Bridge**: connecting to Ecommerce/Retail events for triggers.
- **Bonus Campaign Manager**: Handling limited-time "Double Points" events.
- **Referral & Social Hub**: rewarding users for platform growth and sharing.

## 3. Rewards & Redemption
- **Universal Reward Catalog**: Searchable repository of available prizes.
- **Coupon & Voucher Factory**: Generating unique, trackable discount codes.
- **Checkout Integration**: allowing seamless redemption during the buy flow.
- **Partner Reward Bridge**: (Optional) redeeming points for 3rd party services.

## 4. Operation & Automation
- **Admin Command Center**: Complete control over rules and manual points.
- **Engagement Automation Hub**: triggering rewards for birthdays/milestones.
- **Notification Gateway**: syncing with CRM for personalized reward alerts.
- **Manual Adjustment Audit**: permanent logs for all administrative actions.

## 5. Security & Trust
- **Fraud & Manipulation Shield**: detecting and blocking bad actors.
- **points Liability Guard**: technical controls to prevent over-allocation.
- **Compliance & Privacy Guard**: managing data in accordance with local laws.
- **Account Verification Suite**: ensuring rewards go to legitimate customers.

## 6. Intelligence & Impact
- **Executive Loyalty Dashboard**: Analyzing the true ROI of the program.
- **Predictive Churn Model**: identifying users likely to disengage.
- **LTV Analytics Suite**: measuring the impact of loyalty on customer spend.
- **A/B Testing for Rewards**: optimizing reward types based on performance.
