# 03 - Membership System Modules

## Member Self-Service Portal
A secure area where members can update their profiles, manage their plans, view invoices, and access their digital membership card.

## Administrative Command Center
The central hub for staff to manage member records, configure billing plans, and view attendance reports.

## Subscription & Billing Engine
The financial core that handles PCI-compliant storage of payment methods and executes recurring charges.

## IoT Entry Synchronization Bridge
A real-time listener that syncs digital membership status with physical gate/door controllers.

## Marketing & Engagement Engine
Automates the delivery of "Retention Campaigns" based on member behavior (e.g., "We miss you" emails if they haven't visited in 14 days).
