# 16 - Membership Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Membership Governance
- **Global Plan Configuration**: Defining tiers, prices, and feature sets.
- **Entitlement Master**: Central record of what each membership tier can access.
- **Role-Based Permissions**: granular access control linked to membership level.
- **Family & Group Plan Manager**: handling primary and linked sub-accounts.

## 2. Subscription Lifecycle
- **Recurring Billing Engine**: handling automated charges and payment failures.
- **Proration & Adjustment Hub**: managing upgrades, downgrades, and refunds.
- **Dunning Management System**: professional payment recovery workflows.
- **Automated Lifecycle Alerts**: notifications for renewals, failures, and updates.

## 3. Access & Delivery
- **Real-time Entitlement Middleware**: enforcing access rules in the app stack.
- **Secure Content Gateway**: protecting digital assets from unauthorized access.
- **API Access & Scoping**: managing membership limits for developers (optional).
- **Physical Access Bridge**: (Optional) integration with IoT for facility access.

## 4. Perks & Engagement
- **Benefits Redemption Engine**: Tracking usage of specialized member perks.
- **Member Directory Service**: private hub for networking and discovery.
- **Community Interaction Tools**: messaging and forums for members.
- **automated onboarding flow**: personalized welcome and tutorials for new members.

## 5. Operations & Finance
- **Sales & Revenue Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of MRR and Expansion revenue.
- **Compliance & Privacy Suite**: managing GDPR/CCPA data for members.
- **Tax & Invoice Orchestrator**: generating professional, multi-regional receipts.
- **Member Support Helpdesk**: specialized queue for membership-related issues.

## 6. Intelligence & Growth
- **Churn Analytics Dashboard**: identifying trends in membership cancellations.
- **Cohort Performance Reports**: analyzing retention based on sign-up time.
- **Personalized Offer Engine**: targeting members with upgrades or save-offers.
- **Strategic Growth AI**: predicting future revenue based on current trends.
