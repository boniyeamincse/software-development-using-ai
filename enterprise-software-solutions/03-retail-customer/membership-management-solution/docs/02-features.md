# 02 - Membership Core Features

## 1. Flexible Plan Management
- Support for various tiers (e.g., Basic, Premium, VIP).
- Customizable billing cycles (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly).
- One-time initiation fees and "Add-on" services (e.g., Locker rental, Trainer access).

## 2. Digital Membership Cards
- Mobile-first digital cards with dynamic QR codes for physical access.
- Support for Apple Wallet and Google Pay integration.

## 3. Recurring Billing & Dunning
- Automated payment collection with retry logic for failed transactions (Dunning).
- Transparent invoicing and payment history available in the member portal.

## 4. Physical Access Control (IoT)
- Real-time integration with RFID turnstiles and QR code scanners.
- Automatic access revocation upon payment failure or plan expiration.

## 5. Member Communication & CRM
- Multi-channel alerts (SMS, In-app, Email) for renewal reminders and announcements.
- Integrated survey tools to measure member satisfaction.

## 6. Family & Group Accounts
- Ability for a primary member to manage multiple sub-accounts (e.g., Family plans).
- Shared or individual resource allotments.
