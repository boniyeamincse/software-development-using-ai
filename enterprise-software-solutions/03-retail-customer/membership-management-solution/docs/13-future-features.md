# 13 - Membership Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Using AI to suggest membership discounts for members at high risk of churning.
- **Biometric Entry**: Integration with face-recognition or fingerprint hardware for card-less entry.

## Integrated Marketplace
- **Internal Currency**: "Member Credits" that can be used at the juice bar, pro-shop, or for specialized classes.
- **Social "Buddy" System**: Allowing members to link profiles and see when their friends are currently "In the Club."

## Smart-Facility Logic
- **Occupancy Management**: Real-time facility occupancy display on the member portal (e.g., "The gym is currently at 40% capacity").
- **Smart Locker Integration**: Assigning lockers automatically via the member app upon arrival.
