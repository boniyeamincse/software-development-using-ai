# 03 - Restaurant System Modules

## Front-of-House (FOH) Terminal
The high-speed interface for servers and bartenders to enter orders, process payments, and manage floor seating.

## Kitchen Display System (KDS) Module
A robust, heat-resistant tablet interface for chefs to view, prioritize, and clear active orders.

## Inventory & Stockroom Hub
The back-office module for receiving shipments, tracking waste, and managing vendor relationships.

## Online Order Orchestrator
A middleware service that consolidates orders from the website and 3rd-party delivery apps into the FOH terminal.

## Enterprise Analytics Engine
Generates deep-dive reports on "Best Selling Items," "Labor Cost Percentage," and "Table Turnover Rate."
