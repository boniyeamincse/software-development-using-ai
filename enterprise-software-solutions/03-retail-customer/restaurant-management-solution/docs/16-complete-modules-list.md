# 16 - Restaurant Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Front-of-House (FOH)
- **Interactive Floor Plan**: Real-time management of tables and sections.
- **Reservation & Hostess Stand**: handling bookings, waitlists, and seating.
- **Table-Side POS Client**: optimized interface for servers on tablets/phones.
- **Contactless QR Ordering**: Customer-led ordering and payment gateway.

## 2. Kitchen & Operations (BOH)
- **Kitchen Display System (KDS)**: Course-aware order management for chefs.
- **Recipe & Menu Manager**: Central control for dishes, modifiers, and prices.
- **Inventory & Waste Tracker**: managing raw ingredients and theoretical usage.
- **Vendor & Purchase Order Hub**: automated re-ordering based on stock levels.

## 3. Financial & Staffing
- **Payment & Split-Check Engine**: Complex bill-splitting and gratuity handling.
- **Shift & Roster Builder**: Employee scheduling and labor cost tracking.
- **Tip & Payout Orchestrator**: Logic for pooling and distributing tips.
- **Cash Management Vault**: Tracking physical cash drawers and deposits.

## 4. Customer & Marketing
- **Gift Card & Voucher Factory**: Generating and validating digital/physical cards.
- **Loyalty & Rewards Bridge**: Integrated points-based member benefits.
- **Feedback & Review Connector**: Capturing guest sentiment post-visit.
- **CRM & Guest Preferences**: profiles for regular guest needs and allergies.

## 5. Intelligence & Admin
- **Operations BI Dashboard**: measuring handle times, revenue, and labor.
- **Menu Engineering Suite**: Profitability analysis for menu optimization.
- **Audit & Security Shield**: Monitoring POS deletions and high-risk actions.
- **Multi-Location Hub**: Central control for restaurant chains and franchises.

## 6. Integrations & specialized
- **Third-Party Delivery Bridge**: Centralizing orders from UberEats/DoorDash.
- **Accounting software Sync**: (Optional) syncing daily totals with Quickbooks.
- **IoT Facility Monitor**: (Optional) tracking fridge temps and power usage.
- **AI Inventory Predictor**: forecasting ingredient needs to reduce waste.
