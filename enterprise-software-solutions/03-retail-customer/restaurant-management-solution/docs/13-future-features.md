# 13 - Restaurant Future Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI Upsell Recommendations**: Suggesting specific drinks or desserts to servers based on the table's current order.
- **Computer Vision Kitchen**: Using cameras to detect if a plate is "Service-Ready" and automatically bumping the ticket.
- **Voice-to-Order Drive Thru**: AI-powered natural language ordering for QSR.

## Sustainability & Logistics
- **Waste Reduction AI**: Predicting ingredient spoilage and suggesting "Special of the Day" to clear inventory.
- **Self-Driving Delivery In-House**: Integration with sidewalk robots for immediate local delivery.

## Modern Payments
- **Crypto-Tab**: Allowing guests to settle checks with stablecoins.
- **Pay-by-Face**: Biometric payment integration for frequent VIP members.
