# 08 - Restaurant UI Pages

## 1. Table-Side Ordering (Tablet)
- High-contrast, large-button grid of menu items.
- "Modifier Pop-ups" (e.g., Rare/Medium-Rare, No Onion) to prevent errors.

## 2. Kitchen Display (The "Grid")
- Categorized view of active orders: "Fryer," "Grill," "Salad."
- Timer alerts that turn tickets YELLOW (near SLA) or RED (late).

## 3. Floor Plan View
- Interactive map showing table timers (How long they have been sitting).
- Color coding: Green (Empty), Blue (Seated), Orange (Check Printed).

## 4. Recipe & Cost Management
- Drag-and-drop tool to link raw SKU inventory to menu items.
- Line-graph showing "Ideal vs. Actual" food cost trends.

## 5. End-of-Day (EOD) Report
- Rapid reconciliation of cash, card tips, and total sales.
- Automated "Z-Report" generation and emailing to management.
