# 13 - Ecommerce Future Roadmap

## AI-Driven Retail
- **Personalized Discovery**: AI models that predict customer preferences based on browsing history.
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Real-time price adjustments based on competitor data and demand.

## Immersive Shopping
- **AR Virtual Try-On**: Allowing customers to visualize products (furniture/clothing) in their space.
- **Social Commerce**: Integrated "Live Shopping" modules for streaming platforms.

## Sustainability
- **Eco-Fulfillment**: Algorithms to minimize carbon footprint by optimizing shipping distances.
- **Circular Economy**: Built-in "Resale" or "Trade-in" modules for pre-owned items.
