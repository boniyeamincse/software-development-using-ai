# 16 - Ecommerce Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Storefront & Catalog
- **Advanced Product Indexer**: Handling a massive catalog with sub-second search.
- **Dynamic Content CMS**: managing banners, landing pages, and blogs.
- **Attribute & Variant Manager**: Handling complex product options (SKUs).
- **SEO & Sitemaps Hub**: Automated metadata and structured data generation.

## 2. Shopping Experience
- **Distributed Cart Engine**: fast, reliable, and persistent user baskets.
- **discount & Promo Logic**: Managing coupons, auto-discounts, and bundles.
- **Loyalty & Rewards Bridge**: Integrated points-based incentives.
- **Product Review System**: Trust-building through verified user feedback.

## 3. Checkout & Payment
- **Secure Payment Orchestrator**: Handling global gateways and currencies.
- **Tax & Compliance Service**: Real-time tax calculation and legal compliance.
- **Guest Checkout Hub**: reducing friction for first-time buyers.
- **Subscriptions & Billing**: Handling recurring revenue and memberships.

## 4. Fulfillment & Logistics
- **Smart Inventory Controller**: Multi-warehouse stock tracking and soft-allocations.
- **Carrier Shipping Gateway**: Integration with UPS, FedEx, DHL, and local couriers.
- **Order Tracking Engine**: Real-time updates for customers and admins.
- **Wholesale/B2B Portal**: Specialized pricing and bulk-order workflows.

## 5. Operations & Customer Care
- **Admin Command Center**: Complete control over orders, users, and settings.
- **Customer Support Desk**: Integrated ticketing and live chat.
- **Return & Refund Manager (RMA)**: Automated workflows for reverse logistics.
- **Vendor/Seller Marketplace**: Multi-vendor management tools (optional module).

## 6. Intelligence & Security
- **Conversion Analytics Suite**: Deep insights into the customer journey.
- **AI Fraud Shield**: Protecting against payment and account abuse.
- **Security & Privacy Guard**: GDPR/CCPA and PCI-DSS compliance modules.
- **Personalization Engine**: AI-driven product and content recommendations.
