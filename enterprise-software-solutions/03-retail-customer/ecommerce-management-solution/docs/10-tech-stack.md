# 10 - Ecommerce Technology Stack

## Core Engineering
- **Frontend**: Next.js (for SEO and fast initial load).
- **Backend API**: Node.js (NestJS) or Go.
- **Microservices Orchestration**: Kubernetes (EKS/GKE).

## Specialized Data
- **Search**: Algolia or Elasticsearch.
- **Analytics**: Mixpanel or Google Analytics 4.
- **Cache**: Redis Cluster.

## Payment & Logistics
- **Payments**: Stripe, Adyen, or Braintree.
- **Logistics**: ShipStation or EasyPost.
- **Notifications**: SendGrid (Transactional Email) & Firebase (Mobile Push).
