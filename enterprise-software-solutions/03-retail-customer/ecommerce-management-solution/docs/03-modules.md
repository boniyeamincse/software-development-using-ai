# 03 - Ecommerce System Modules

## Merchant Operations Portal
The "Back Office" for administrators to manage products, view sales reports, and handle global configurations.

## Customer Front-Store
The customer-facing application (Web/Mobile) optimized for speed and conversion. Includes wishlist, reviews, and search.

## Seller Dashboard (Marketplace)
A self-service module for 3rd party vendors to manage their own inventory, orders, and financial statements.

## Logistics & Fulfillment Module
Integration with shipping carriers (FedEx, DHL, UPS) to generate labels and track shipments in real-time.

## Support & CRM Module
Ticketing system for handling returns, refunds, and customer inquiries. Includes purchase history for support agents.

## Marketing & SEO Engine
Tools for managing meta-tags, sitemaps, and promotional banners. Includes integration with email marketing platforms.
