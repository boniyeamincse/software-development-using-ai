# 02 - Ecommerce Core Features

## 1. Catalog & Product Management
- Support for physical, digital, and subscription-based products.
- Robust attribute system (Size, Color, Material) with variant pricing.
- Bulk import/export via CSV/JSON.

## 2. Dynamic Shopping Cart & Checkout
- "Smart" cart with persistent sessions across devices.
- Guest checkout support.
- Real-time tax and shipping calculations.

## 3. Order & Inventory Management
- Automated stock decrement on purchase and increment on return.
- "Low Stock" alerts and backorder management.
- Order status tracking (Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Canceled).

## 4. Promotion & Loyalty Engine
- Flexible coupon system (Percentage, Fixed Amount, BOGO).
- Points-based loyalty programs and tiered memberships.

## 5. Multi-Vendor Marketplace Support
- Dedicated seller portals for product listing and payout tracking.
- Commission management and automated split-payments.

## 6. Globalization
- Multi-currency support with real-time exchange rate updates.
- Multi-language localization for global reach.
