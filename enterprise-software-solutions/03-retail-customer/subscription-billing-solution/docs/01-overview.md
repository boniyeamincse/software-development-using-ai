# 01 - Subscription Billing Overview

## Introduction
The **Subscription Billing Solution** is an enterprise-grade financial engine designed to manage the complexities of recurring revenue. It supports diverse billing models, from simple monthly flat-rates to complex usage-based consumption.

## The Recurring Revenue Challenge
Transitioning to or managing a subscription business introduces unique financial hurdles:
- **Revenue Recognition**: Correctly accounting for deferred revenue over the service period.
- **Invoicing at Scale**: Generating thousands of accurate invoices simultaneously without manual intervention.
- **Dunning Management**: Gracefully handling credit card failures to prevent involuntary churn.
- **Pricing Agility**: The need to launch new plans and promotions quickly without rewriting code.

## The Solution
A robust, cloud-native billing orchestrator that automates the entire "Order-to-Cash" lifecycle. It features a flexible product catalog, automated tax calculation, multi-currency support, and a comprehensive dunning system, all while maintaining strict PCI and GAAP compliance.

## Core Objectives
- **Maximum Flexibility**: Support for Tiered, Volume, and Per-unit pricing models.
- **Automated Operations**: Hands-off invoicing, payment collection, and revenue recognition.
- **Churn Reduction**: Intelligent dunning and retry logic to salvage failed payments.
- **Financial Integrity**: Comprehensive audit trails and reporting for accounting reconciliation.
