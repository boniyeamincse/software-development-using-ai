# 03 - Subscription System Modules

## Product Catalog Manager
The administrative interface for defining plans, add-ons, and pricing tiers.

## Subscription Lifecycle Engine
The core service that manages the "State" of every subscription (Active, Trialling, Past Due, Canceled).

## Metering & Usage Hub
A high-throughput ingestion point for receiving usage data (API calls, data storage, etc.) used for consumption-based billing.

## Customer Billing Portal
A self-service area where customers can upgrade plans, view invoice history, and manage payment methods.

## Revenue Analytics Suite
Generates critical SaaS metrics: MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), ARR, Churn Rate, and LTV (Lifetime Value).
