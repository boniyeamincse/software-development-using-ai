# 02 - Billing Core Features

## 1. Multi-Model Pricing Engine
- Support for Flat-rate, Per-Seat, Tiered, and Volume-based pricing.
- Usage-based billing: Invoicing based on metered data sent via API.
- "Freemium" to "Paid" transition management.

## 2. Global Tax & Compliance
- Automated VAT/GST and Sales Tax calculation based on customer location (integration with Avalara/TaxJar).
- Support for 135+ currencies and local payment methods.

## 3. Automated Invoicing
- Professional, white-labeled PDF invoices generated and emailed automatically.
- Support for "Consolidated Billing" (One invoice for multiple subscriptions).

## 4. Intelligent Dunning System
- Customizable retry schedules for failed credit card transactions.
- Automated "Grace Period" management before service suspension.
- Secure "Update Payment Method" links sent in dunning emails.

## 5. Coupon & Discount Orchestration
- Percentage-based, Flat-amount, and "First Month Free" coupons.
- Referral-code tracking and automated discount application.

## 6. Revenue Recognition (GAAP/IFRS)
- Automated splitting of revenue into recognized and deferred categories.
- Integration with accounting software like NetSuite, QuickBooks, or Xero.
