# 08 - Subscription UI Pages

## 1. Executive Revenue Dashboard
- Real-time charts for MRR, Churn, and ARPU (Average Revenue Per User).
- "Cohort Analysis" showing long-term retention trends.

## 2. Product Catalog Builder
- Interface for setting up complex tiered pricing (e.g., First 100 units at $1, next at $0.50).
- "Feature Flag" mapping tool to link plans to app features.

## 3. Customer Billing Timeline
- A searchable, filterable view of every customer's billing events: "Card Added -> Trial Started -> Payment 1 Success -> Upgraded."

## 4. Dunning Strategy Designer
- Drag-and-drop workflow tool for defining what happens when a payment fails (Day 1: Email, Day 3: Retri, Day 7: Suspend).

## 5. Self-Service Portal (User)
- Clean, simple interface for changing credit cards, downloading receipts, and switching plans.
