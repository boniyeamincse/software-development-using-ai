# 16 - Subscription Billing Complete Modules List

## 1. Plan & Product Governance
- **Global Plan Master**: Defining tiers, cycles, and multi-currency pricing.
- **Plan Versioning Controller**: managing legacy and current plan structures.
- **feature Entitlement Bridge**: linking billing status to application feature flags.
- **Add-on & Usage Module**: managing one-time charges and metered billing.

## 2. Recurring Billing Operations
- **System Billing Clock**: automated execution of recurring charges.
- **Multi-Gateway Orchestrator**: Routing payments through Stripe, Adyen, etc.
- **Dunning & Recovery Suite**: handling failed payments and debt recovery.
- **Proration & Adjustment Engine**: handling mid-cycle plan changes accurately.

## 3. Compliance & Finance
- **Universal Tax Service**: Real-time tax calculation for global regions.
- **Invoice & Receipt Factory**: Generating professional, trackable billing docs.
- **Refund & Credit Manager**: handling financial reversals and account balances.
- **Acounting Export Bridge**: syncing billing data with ERP/Accounting software.

## 4. User Self-Service
- **Branded Billing Portal**: Secure area for users to manage their payments.
- **Payment Method Vault**: Secure (tokenized) handling of credit cards/banks.
- **Subscription History Log**: Complete audit trail of all plan movements.
- **Magic Link Auth Gateway**: providing frictionless, secure portal access.

## 5. Operations & Security
- **Admin Subscription Command**: Manual control for support and ops teams.
- **Dunning Policy Builder**: customizing retry and notification rules.
- **Revenue Recognition Shield**: ensuring compliance with IFRS-15/ASC-606.
- **PII & PCI Privacy Guard**: protecting sensitive billing and identity data.

## 6. Intelligence & Reporting
- **MRR/ARR Executive Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of growth metrics.
- **Churn & Retention Suite**: Deep insights into user lifecycle trends.
- **AI Price Optimizer**: Data-driven recommendations for revenue growth.
- **Financial Audit Vault**: Permanent record of every billing interaction.
