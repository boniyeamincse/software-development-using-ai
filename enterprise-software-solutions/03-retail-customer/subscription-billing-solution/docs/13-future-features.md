# 13 - Subscription Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Predictive Churn Engine**: AI that analyzes payment history and usage patterns to identify customers about to cancel.
- **Dynamic Pricing AI**: Automatically adjusting prices based on demand, user-segments, or regional purchasing power.

## Modern Economy
- **Crypto-Subscription**: Direct support for recurring payments using stablecoins and smart-contracts.
- **Interoperable Billing**: One subscription that grants access to a network of different partner applications (Bundle-as-a-Service).

## Financial Tools
- **Automated Tax Filing**: One-click filing for multi-state sales tax and international VAT directly from the billing dashboard.
- **AR Financing Integration**: Using the platform's predictable ARR data to unlock instant working-capital loans.
