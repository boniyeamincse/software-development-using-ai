# 13 - Coupon Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Dynamic Offer AI**: Real-time generation of custom discount values for users based on their probability of conversion (e.g., If user is about to leave, offer 15%, otherwise offer 5%).
- **Predictive Budgeting**: ML models that predict total campaign spend based on social media trends and historic data.

## Integrated Marketplaces
- **Influencer Tracking 2.0**: Deep integration with social APIs to track clicks-to-codes-to-revenue in real-time.
- **Retail-Media Integration**: Letting brands "Sponsor" specific discount codes within a retailer's marketplace.

## Modern Experiences
- **Single-Use QR Scans**: Unique QR codes for physical stores that are instantly revoked upon scanning to prevent re-use.
- **Voice-Assistant Discounts**: "Alexa, check for my exclusive daily deal."
