# 01 - Coupon & Discount Overview

## Introduction
The **Coupon & Discount Solution** is a highly flexible marketing engine designed to drive sales and customer acquisition through advanced promotional logic. It serves as the "Incentive Layer" for e-commerce and retail platforms.

## The Promotion Challenge
Managing discounts at scale often leads to:
- **Coupon Abuse**: Users sharing single-use codes or stacking multiple discounts.
- **Profit Margin Erosion**: Lack of guardrails on deep discounts.
- **Complexity**: Difficulty in creating specific rules (e.g., "Buy 2 of X, Get 30% off Y, but only for Gold Members").
- **Performance Lag**: Slow checkout times caused by heavy promotion calculation logic.

## The Solution
A high-speed, rules-based engine that evaluates discounts in real-time. It supports unique one-time codes, automatic "Cart-Level" discounts, and complex multi-item bundles. It features a robust administrative portal for marketing teams to launch, monitor, and cap campaigns with ease.

## Core Objectives
- **Granular Control**: Rules-based logic for targeting specific items, categories, or user segments.
- **Prevention of Abuse**: Intelligent tracking of code usage and IP-based restrictions.
- **Real-Time Performance**: Sub-millisecond discount calculations even during peak sales.
- **Actionable Insights**: Tracking ROI and conversion rates for every active campaign.
