# 16 - Coupon & Discount Complete Modules List

## 1. Discount Governance
- **Universal Discount Factory**: Defining percentages, fixed amounts, and free shipping.
- **Conflict Resolution Engine**: managing rule precedence for multiple coupons.
- **Campaign Master**: Grouping discounts into marketing initiatives.
- **Multi-Currency Pricing**: handling discount values across different regions.

## 2. Code Management
- **Unique Code Generator**: mass-generating alphanumeric codes with check-digits.
- **High-Performance Lookup**: sub-millisecond validation of input codes.
- **Dynamic Link (URL) Manager**: auto-applying discounts via specific URLs.
- **Physical QR/Barcode Bridge**: enabling coupons for in-person retail.

## 3. Eligibility & rules
- **Contextual Rule Engine**: validating cart contents, user level, and value.
- **Inclusion/Exclusion Hub**: fine-grained control over SKUs and Categories.
- **Usage Limit Controller**: managing single-use, recurring, or global limits.
- **Geo-fencing & Time Hub**: activation based on location and schedule.

## 4. Marketing & engagement
- **Referral Reward Bridge**: Automated discount distribution for platform growth.
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Proactive 'Save Offers' for high-value carts.
- **Loyalty & Rewards Link**: Redeeming points for specialized discount codes.
- **First-Purchase Incentives**: automated welcome offers for new users.

## 5. Operations & Security
- **Promotion Command Center**: Admin dashboard for managing all activations.
- **Abuse & Fraud Shield**: protecting against brute-force and multi-account gaming.
- **Audit & History Log**: Permanent record of every discount creation and use.
- **Rate-Limiting Gateway**: protecting the engine from excessive requests.

## 6. Intelligence & Impact
- **Margin Integrity Dashboard**: analyzing the impact of discounts on profit.
- **Campaign ROI Suite**: Performance metrics for different marketing channels.
- **AI Discount Recommender**: predicting the minimal discount needed to convert.
- **A/B Testing for Promotions**: testing different offers on specific user groups.
