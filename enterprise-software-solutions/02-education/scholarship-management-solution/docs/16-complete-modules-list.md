# 16 - Scholarship Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Governance & Fund Setup
- **Tutorized Funding Manager**: Managing the financial source for each scholarship.
- **Eligibility Engine Builder**: Creating complex rules for awards.
- **Donor Hub**: managing relationships and reporting for scholarship sponsors.

## 2. applicant lifecycle
- **Prospective applicant Portal**: Single entry point for all scholarship seekers.
- **Intelligent Application Engine**: tailored forms that adapt based on applicant data.
- **Document Management System**: Secure, OCR-ready transcript and income proof storage.
- **applicant Communication Gateway**: Multi-channel status and requirement alerts.

## 3. Review & Selection
- **Blind Review Orchestrator**: Anonymizing data for unbiased evaluations.
- **Committee Matcher**: Assigning applications to reviewers based on expertise.
- **Selection Decision Hub**: Capturing committee scores, notes, and final votes.
- **Rubric & Score Engine**: Standardizing qualitative assessment.

## 4. Selection & Financial Operations
- **Award Management Suite**: Managing the final list of recipients and award amounts.
- **Financial Aid (SIS) Integration**: Automating tuition credits and payouts.
- **Compliance & Renewal Monitor**: Tracking ongoing eligibility of recipients.
- **Disbursement Audit Trail**: Permanent records of every dollar awarded.

## 5. Intelligence & Outcome
- **Impact Analytics**: Measuring graduation and career success of scholars.
- **Diversity & Equity Dashboard**: analyzing award distribution across demographics.
- **Donor Impact Reporting**: specialized summaries for fund sponsors.

## 6. Security & Support
- **applicant Identity Verification**: Ensuring valid student status.
- **External Data Bridge**: Checking third-party financial records if available.
- **applicant Support Helpdesk**: dedicated queue for scholarship questions.
