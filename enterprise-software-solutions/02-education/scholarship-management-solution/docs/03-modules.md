# 03 - Scholarship Modules

## Student Opportunity Portal
A public-facing portal where students can search, filter, and apply for open scholarships. Includes an "Application Status" tracker.

## Reviewer & Committee Dashboard
A secure workspace for faculty or board members to score applications, leave comments, and participate in selection votes.

## Financial Aid & Admin Office
The master portal for setting up new funds, managing eligibility rules, and executing disbursements.

## Donor Impact Portal
An isolated view for scholarship donors to track the distribution of their funds and communicate with scholarship recipients.

## Compliance & Reporting Engine
Generates government and institutional reports regarding fund allocation, demographic distribution, and merit audits.
