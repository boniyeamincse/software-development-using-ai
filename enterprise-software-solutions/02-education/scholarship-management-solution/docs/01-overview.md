# 01 - Scholarship Management Overview

## Introduction
The **Scholarship Management Solution** is a specialized financial aid platform designed to streamline the lifecycle of educational grants, bursaries, and scholarships.

## The Problem
Managing scholarships manually or via generic tools leads to:
- **Application Fatigue**: Students struggle with complex, non-standardized forms.
- **Biased Review**: Lack of blinded review processes can lead to unfairness.
- **Fund Leakage**: Difficulty in tracking multi-year disbursements and maintaining donor compliance.
- **Manual Verification**: Intensive labor required to verify grades and financial eligibility.

## The Solution
An automated, transparent portal that handles everything from donor fund management to student disbursement. It includes AI-assisted eligibility checking and a centralized portal for reviewers.

## Core Objectives
- **Standardize Applications**: A unified interface for all institutional and external funds.
- **Ensure Fairness**: Blinded review workflows and merit-based ranking algorithms.
- **Track Disbursements**: Real-time integration with student accounts and accounting systems.
- **Donor Engagement**: Reporting tools to show donors the direct impact of their contributions.
