# 13 - Scholarship Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **AI Essay Analysis**: Providing reviewers with a "Coherence & Originality" score.
- **Automated Matching**: Recommending scholarships to students based on their profile data using ML.

## Marketplace & Portability
- **Scholarship Ledger (Blockchain)**: An immutable record of awards that students can share with future employers.
- **Global Grant Search**: Integrated API with external scholarship boards (e.g., Fastweb/Chegg).

## Modern Payments
- **Crypto Disbursement**: Support for stablecoin payments for international scholarships.
- **Micro-Grants**: Tools for "Emergency Grants" (fast-tracked grants for students in immediate crisis).
