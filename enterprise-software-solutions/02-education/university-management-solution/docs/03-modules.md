# 03 - University System Modules

## Faculty Module
Empowers Deans and Professors to manage course materials, research budgets, and student evaluations within their specific departments.

## Registrar Module
The "Engine Room" of the university. Handles records, transcripts, degree audits, and graduation ceremonies.

## Admissions & Financial Aid
Manages the influx of applications and the complex logic of scholarship eligibility and merit-based aid.

## Research Management
Dedicated space for tracking laboratory equipment, grant funds, and inter-institutional collaborations.

## Student Life Portal
Focuses on non-academic aspects: Clubs, sports, residential (Dorm) management, and career counseling.

## Alumni & Development
Tools for fundraising campaigns, alumni networks, and career tracking of graduates.
