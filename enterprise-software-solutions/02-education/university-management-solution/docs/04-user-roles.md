# 04 - University Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level | Primary Responsibilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vice-Chancellor / Rector** | Strategic Access | Institution-wide reports and high-level financial audits. |
| **Dean** | Departmental Access | Academic oversight of a specific faculty or school. |
| **Professor** | Instructional Access | Research management, grading, and curriculum design. |
| **Registrar Staff** | Academic Records | Transcripts, enrollment verification, and degree audits. |
| **Accountant** | Financial Access | Tuition collection and research grant disbursement. |
| **Student** | Personal Portal | Course registration, viewing grades, and bursary status. |
| **Alumni** | Networking | Career updates and donation management. |
