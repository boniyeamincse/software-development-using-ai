# 09 - University Workflows

## The Degree Audit Workflow
1. Student requests audit via portal.
2. System fetches all completed credits and current enrollments.
3. Logical engine compares records against the "Plan of Study" for their major.
4. Missing requirements (e.g., "Need 3 more hours of Science Elective") highlighted.
5. Recommendation engine suggests open slots in the upcoming semester.

## Research Grant Approval
1. Faculty submits proposal and budget through the system.
2. Department Head reviews and attaches recommendation.
3. Office of Research checks against compliance and funding availability.
4. Approval/Rejection notification; if approved, new "Project Account" opened automatically.

## Graduation Clearance
1. Registrar triggers batch "End-of-Term" processing.
2. Financial office flags any outstanding debts.
3. System confirms all credit requirements met.
4. "Ready for Graduation" list generated for the Chancellor's office.
