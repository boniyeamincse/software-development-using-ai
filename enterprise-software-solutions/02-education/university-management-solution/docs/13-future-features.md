# 13 - University Future Roadmap

## AI & Automation
- **Predictive Retention**: Identifying students at risk of dropping out based on engagement patterns.
- **Automated Grading Assistant**: NLP tools to assist professors with essay feedback.

## Blockchain & Web3
- **Self-Sovereign Identity**: Allowing students to "own" their academic record and share it with employers directly.

## Immersive Learning
- Integrated Lab booking system for VR/AR research facilities.
