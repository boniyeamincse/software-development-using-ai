# 02 - University Core Features

## 1. Credit-Based Course Management
- Modular curriculum design with prerequisites and co-requisites.
- Support for Semester, Trimester, and Year-based academic calendars.

## 2. Dynamic Enrollment & Registration
- Automated "Course Add/Drop" periods with waitlist management.
- Real-time classroom capacity tracking.

## 3. High-Stakes Examination
- Secure online proctoring integration.
- Automated CGPA calculation across multiple grading systems.

## 4. Research & Grants Management
- Tracking grant lifecycles (application, approval, disbursement).
- Repository for faculty publications and intellectual property.

## 5. Faculty & HR Management
- Portfolios for faculty tenure tracking.
- Workload management (Research vs. Teaching hours).

## 6. Financial Services
- Complex fee structures (International vs. Domestic tuition).
- Integration with scholarship and financial aid disbursements.
