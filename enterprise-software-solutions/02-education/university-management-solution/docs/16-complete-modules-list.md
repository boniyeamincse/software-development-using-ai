# 16 - University Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Governance & Configuration
- **Multi-Campus Manager**: Central control for multiple institutional locations.
- **Academic Calendar Engine**: Managing semesters, exam periods, and holidays.
- **Policy & Compliance Hub**: Enforcing university-wide regulations and standards.

## 2. Core Academic Management
- **Department & Faculty Directory**: Organization of academic units and staff.
- **Program & Curriculum Builder**: Defining degree requirements and credit structures.
- **Course Catalog Service**: Centralized repository of all academic offerings.

## 3. Student Lifecycle
- **Adaptive Admission Portal**: Intelligent onboarding for prospective students.
- **Smart Registration Engine**: Handling high-volume course enrollment.
- **Student Information System (SIS)**: The master record for all student data.
- **Graduation & Alumni Manager**: Tracking the transition from student to alumnus.

## 4. Learning & Assessment
- **LMS Integration Bridge**: Seamless connectivity with external platforms (Canvas/Moodle).
- **Examination Control Center**: Managing exam schedules, proctoring, and seat plans.
- **Automated Grading & CGPA Engine**: Precise calculation of academic standing.
- **Digital Credentials Vault**: Issuing and verifying degrees and transcripts.

## 5. Research & specialized Services
- **Grant & Funding Manager**: Lifecycle tracking of research finances.
- **Institutional Research Repository**: Hosting internal publications and IP.
- **Professional Development Tracker**: Managing faculty certifications and growth.

## 6. Operations & Finance
- **Student Billing & Accounts**: Managing tuition, fines, and payment plans.
- **Faculty Payroll & Workload**: Integrating teaching hours with HR systems.
- **Facility & Resource Scheduler**: Managing classrooms, labs, and equipment.

## 7. Intelligence & Communication
- **Executive Intelligence Dashboard**: Real-time KPIs for university leadership.
- **Unified Messaging Gateway**: Multi-channel alerts (Email, SMS, App).
- **Predictive Student Analytics**: Identifying risk and opportunity early.
