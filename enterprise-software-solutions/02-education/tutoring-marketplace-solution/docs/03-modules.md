# Tutoring Marketplace Solution - Modules

## 1. Module Summary

The platform can be divided into 18 core modules.

## 2. Core Modules

1. Identity and Authentication
2. User Profile Management
3. Tutor Verification
4. Tutor Catalog and Discovery
5. Matching and Recommendations
6. Availability Management
7. Booking Management
8. Session Delivery
9. Messaging and Notifications
10. Content and Resource Sharing
11. Payments
12. Payouts and Finance
13. Ratings and Reviews
14. Support and Disputes
15. Compliance and Audit
16. Reporting and Analytics
17. Promotions and Growth
18. Partner Institution Management

## 3. Dependency Notes

- Booking depends on identity, profiles, availability, and payments.
- Payouts depend on completed sessions, finance rules, and compliance checks.
- Reviews depend on completed sessions and fraud controls.
- Analytics consumes events from discovery, booking, sessions, and revenue modules.
