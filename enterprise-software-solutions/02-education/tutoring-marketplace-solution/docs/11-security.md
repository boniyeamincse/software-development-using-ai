# Tutoring Marketplace Solution - Security

## 1. Security Objectives

- Protect learner and tutor personal data.
- Prevent fraudulent bookings, reviews, and payouts.
- Enforce role-based and policy-based access.
- Maintain auditability for regulated and trust-sensitive actions.

## 2. Control Areas

1. MFA for admins and high-risk tutor actions
2. Encrypted PII and document storage
3. Signed URLs for sensitive media
4. Content moderation and abuse reporting
5. Rate limiting and bot protection
6. Secure payment tokenization
7. Immutable audit logs for finance and compliance actions

## 3. Special Considerations

- Minor safety and guardian consent controls
- Credential fraud detection for tutor verification
- Review manipulation detection
- Session privacy and meeting access restrictions
