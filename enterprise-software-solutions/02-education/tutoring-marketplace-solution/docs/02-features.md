# Tutoring Marketplace Solution - Features

## 1. Feature Overview

This document groups the tutoring marketplace into the main business capabilities needed for an MVP and later expansion.

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1 User Registration and Identity
- Student, guardian, tutor, and admin account creation
- Email, phone, and social sign-in
- KYC and tutor verification submission

### 2.2 Tutor Profile Management
- Bio, qualifications, subject expertise, pricing, and availability
- Demo video, portfolio, and teaching preferences
- Profile moderation and publication state

### 2.3 Search and Discovery
- Search by subject, grade, curriculum, language, and exam
- Filters for price, rating, modality, and response time
- Recommended tutors and saved searches

### 2.4 Scheduling and Booking
- Calendar-based slot management
- Instant book or request approval flows
- Recurring plans, bundles, and package booking

### 2.5 Payments and Payouts
- Checkout, invoices, commissions, and settlement tracking
- Tutor wallet and payout requests
- Refunds, reversals, and failed payment recovery

### 2.6 Session Management
- Live link generation for online sessions
- Attendance, session notes, and resource attachments
- Session status lifecycle and completion confirmation

### 2.7 Messaging and Notifications
- In-app chat, email, SMS, and push reminders
- Booking reminders and follow-up notifications
- Safety filters and moderation review

### 2.8 Ratings and Reviews
- Student and guardian ratings
- Review moderation and dispute review
- Tutor quality score aggregation

### 2.9 Learning Progress Tracking
- Goals, milestones, assignments, and tutor notes
- Student progress summary and guardian visibility
- Outcome-based reporting

### 2.10 Admin Operations
- User moderation, fraud review, and support tooling
- Pricing rules, promotions, and category management
- Marketplace health dashboards

### 2.11 Institution Program Support
- Managed tutor pools for partner organizations
- Bulk learner enrollment and coordinator dashboards
- Contracted pricing and reporting

### 2.12 Compliance and Safety
- Minor safety controls and guardian approvals
- Data privacy controls and consent management
- Qualification expiry monitoring
