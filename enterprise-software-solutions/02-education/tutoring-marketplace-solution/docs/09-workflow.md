# Tutoring Marketplace Solution - Workflow

## 1. Primary Workflows

1. Tutor registration and verification
2. Student or guardian onboarding
3. Tutor discovery and shortlist creation
4. Slot selection and booking confirmation
5. Payment authorization and invoice creation
6. Session delivery and attendance capture
7. Session completion and review request
8. Refund, cancellation, and rescheduling
9. Tutor payout processing
10. Dispute and support resolution

## 2. Workflow Notes

- Minor learners should require guardian approval for payments and communications where policy demands it.
- Refund rules should vary by cancellation window and tutor no-show scenarios.
- Payout eligibility should depend on session completion, review state, and fraud checks.
