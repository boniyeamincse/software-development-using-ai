# 13 - Parent Communication Roadmap

## AI-Powered Engagement
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Alerting counselors if parent-teacher communication indicates high levels of stress or dissatisfaction.
- **Auto-Summarization**: Summarizing long chat threads or newsletters into concise 3-bullet points for busy parents.

## Integrated Services
- **School Store Integration**: Allowing parents to buy uniforms or books directly from child-specific notices.
- **Attendance via Face-ID Parent Check-In**: Securely identifying parents at the school gate during pickup.

## Enhanced Media
- **Live Streaming**: Integrated support for viewing graduation ceremonies or sports games directly in the app.
