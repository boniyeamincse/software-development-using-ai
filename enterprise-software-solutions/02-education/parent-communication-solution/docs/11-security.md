# 11 - Parent Communication Security

## Data Privacy & Safety
- **End-to-Edge Encryption**: All private messages encrypted during transit (TLS 1.3).
- **Masked Data**: Student PII is abstracted in threads to prevent data leaks.
- **Proactive Moderation**: AI detection of inappropriate language or harassment in parent-teacher chats.

## Access Control
- **Strict Relationship Validation**: Ensuring a parent can *only* message teachers associated with their child.
- **Revocable Access**: Instant lockout of withdrawn students' parents from the school ecosystem.

## Compliance
- **GDPR / FERPA**: Adherence to international student and family privacy laws.
- **Audit Logging**: Full history of broadcast announcements for institutional accountability.
