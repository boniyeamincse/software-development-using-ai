# 16 - Parent Communication Complete Modules List

## 1. Unified Messaging Platform
- **Multi-Channel Alert Gateway**: reliable delivery via App, SMS, and Email.
- **Parent-Teacher Messenger**: Secure, threaded, and searchable 1:1 chat.
- **Announcement Command Center**: School-wide broadcasts with translation.
- **Read-Receipt & Engagement Analytics**: Monitoring how well families are informed.

## 2. Parent-Teacher Interaction
- **PTM Scheduler**: Conflict-free meeting block booking.
- **Appointment & Support Desk**: Managing parent requests and inquiries.
- **Volunteer & Participation Hub**: Coordinating family involvement in school events.

## 3. Student Progress Transparency
- **Real-Time Grade & Attendance Feed**: Daily snapshots of student standing.
- **automated Progress Digests**: Weekly summaries of achievements and concerns.
- **PDF Report Card Generator**: Professional, secure academic records.
- **homework & Task Monitor**: Ensuring parents can support learning at home.

## 4. Events & Logistics
- **Interactive School Calendar**: Central source of truth for events and holidays.
- **RSVP & Permission Slip Manager**: Handling field trip and event approvals.
- **School News & Media Gallery**: Sharing the school experience through visual content.

## 5. Security & Administration
- **Family Unit Manager**: Mapping complex relationships and authorizations.
- **Consent & Privacy Dashboard**: Managing photorelease and data permissions.
- **Emergency Alert System**: Priority bypass for critical safety notifications.
- **FERPA Compliance Engine**: Scoping access to student data based on legal status.

## 6. Global Reach
- **Language Localization Service**: Translating all communications for diverse families.
- **Accessibility Suite**: Ensuring all parents can access info regardless of ability.
- **Community Outreach Tools**: Connecting with prospective families and partners.
