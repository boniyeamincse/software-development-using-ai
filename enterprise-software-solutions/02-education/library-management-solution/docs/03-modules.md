# 03 - Library System Modules

## Librarian Admin Portal
The "Central Hub" for cataloging, circulation desk operations, and managing library staff. Includes procurement and vendor management tools.

## Student OPAC (Online Public Access Catalog)
The public-facing portal where students search for books, view their loan history, pay fines, and place holds.

## Digital Asset Warehouse
A secure repository for storing and streaming e-books, research papers, and institutional archives.

## Inventory & Audit Module
Handles the logical and physical tracking of assets. Integrates with RFID hardware for high-speed audits.

## Analytics & Insights Engine
Generates reports on "Circulation Density," "Book Popularity," and "Budget Utilization."
