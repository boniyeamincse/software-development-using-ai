# 11 - Library Security & DRM

## Digital Safety
- **DRM (Digital Rights Management)**: Ensuring e-books cannot be downloaded or shared outside the platform.
- **Secure Streaming**: E-content is streamed in chunks to prevent full-file scraping.

## Physical & Operational Security
- **Theft Prevention**: Real-time alerts when a non-checked-out RFID tag passes the exit gate.
- **Data Integrity**: Periodic checksum validation on digital archives to prevent silent corruption.

## Privacy
- **Anonymous Search**: Search history is encrypted and not linked to student PII unless requested by the user.
- **GDPR Compliance**: Tools for students to view or wipe their borrowing history records.
