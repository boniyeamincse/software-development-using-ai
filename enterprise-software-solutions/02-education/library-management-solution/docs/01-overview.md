# 01 - Library Management Overview

## Introduction
The **Library Management Solution** is a modern, digital-first system designed to manage the lifecycle of physical and digital assets in an educational institution.

## The Traditional Library Struggle
Legacy library systems often face:
- **Inventory Mismatch**: Records not reflecting actual shelf count.
- **High Friction**: Slow checkout and return processes causing queues.
- **Digital Gap**: Difficulty in managing e-books, journals, and physical books in one place.
- **Poor Engagement**: Students unaware of new arrivals or overdue penalties until it’s too late.

## The Solution
An integrated platform featuring RFID/Barcode tracking, a unified search (OPAC), and automated circulation logic. It bridges the gap between physical books and digital repositories.

## Core Objectives
- **Automate Circulation**: Self-service kiosks for checkout and returns.
- **Unified Cataloging**: A single source of truth for all physical and digital media.
- **Enhance Discovery**: AI-driven recommendations based on student reading history.
- **Reduce Delinquency**: Automated multi-channel reminders for overdue items.
