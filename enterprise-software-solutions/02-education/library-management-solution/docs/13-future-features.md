# 13 - Library Roadmap

## AI & Future Tech
- **Visual Search**: Snapping a photo of a book cover to find its shelf location or related digital content.
- **AI Recommendation Engine**: "Collaborative Filtering" to suggest books based on what students in the same major are reading.

## Smart Spaces
- **Integrated Study-Room Booking**: Merging library asset management with physical room reservations.
- **Augmented Reality (AR) Wayfinding**: Using the mobile app to show an arrow overlay pointing to the exact book on a shelf.

## Community & Open Science
- **Inter-Library Loan (ILL)**: API integration for sharing assets across multiple institutional libraries.
- **Open Access Repository**: Automated tools for publishing faculty research directly to the library’s digital vault.
