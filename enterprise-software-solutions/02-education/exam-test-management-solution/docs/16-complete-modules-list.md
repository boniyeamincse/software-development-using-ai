# 16 - Exam & Test Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Content & Knowledge Repository
- **Secure Question Bank**: Centralized, encrypted store for all assessment materials.
- **Support for Rich Media**: Integration for images, diagrams, and complex LaTeX equations.
- **Categorization Engine**: Mapping questions to skills, difficulty levels, and syllabus units.

## 2. Examination Planning & Logistics
- **Exam Schedule & Calendar**: Managing dates, times, and deadlines for all assessments.
- **Hall & Facility Manager**: Booking physical exam spaces and equipment.
- **Seat Plan Generator**: Automated distance-optimized student placement.
- **Proctoring Logistics Hub**: Managing staff assignments and check-ins.

## 3. High-Integrity Delivery
- **Timed Online Testing Client**: Robust interface for students with auto-save and low-latency.
- **Safe Browser Bridge**: Enforcement of OS-level lockdowns for high-stakes exams.
- **Proctor Portal & Live Feed**: Monitoring active sessions and managing student issues.
- **AI-Enhanced Integrity Shield**: Real-time detection of suspicious behavior.

## 4. Evaluation & Results
- **Objective Grading Service**: Automated scoring for MCQs and digital input questions.
- **Subjective Marking Workflow**: Managing long-form response grading with rubrics.
- **Moderation & Resolution Suite**: Handling appeals, re-marking, and special cases.
- **Results Compilation Engine**: Calculating final scores, percentiles, and grades.

## 5. Insights & Analytics
- **Student Performance Dashboard**: Individual progress and strength/weakness mapping.
- **Teacher Course Reports**: Aggregated view of how a cohort performed on specific topics.
- **Examination Quality Audit**: Psychometric analysis of question validity and reliability.

## 6. Access & Support
- **Digital Hall Ticket System**: Issuing secure entry permits with biometric verification.
- **Special Accommodations Hub**: Managing time extensions or assistive tools for students.
- **Public Results Portal**: Secure publication of final grades to students and parents.
