# 13 - Exam Future Roadmap

## AI Innovation
- **Adaptive Testing**: Dynamically changing question difficulty based on student's previous answers.
- **Handwriting Assessment**: Using OCR to grade scanned physical papers with AI models.

## Experience Enhancements
- **VR Assessments**: Practical exams conducted in virtual laboratory environments.
- **Emotion Analysis**: Tracking student stress levels during the exam to provide wellness breaks.
