# 01 - Exam System Overview

## Introduction
The **Exam and Test Management Solution** is a secure, high-integrity assessment platform designed to automate the entire examination lifecycle from question generation to result publication.

## Assessment Challenges
Modern educational institutions face several hurdles in assessments:
- **Academic Integrity**: Preventing cheating in both online and offline environments.
- **Grading Efficiency**: Manual grading of hundreds of answer sheets is slow and prone to error.
- **Content Security**: Protecting question papers from leaks.
- **Data Analytics**: Lack of deep insights into student performance trends.

## The Solution
A robust digital ecosystem that supports multiple exam formats (MCQ, Descriptive, Practical), integrates with Proctoring AI, and provides instant analytics for stakeholders.

## Core Objectives
- **Ensure Integrity**: Secure browsers and AI monitoring.
- **Automate Grading**: OMR scanning and auto-marking for objective tests.
- **Scalability**: Support for high-concurrency entrance exams with thousands of simultaneous users.
- **Accessibility**: Multi-language support and ADA-compliant interfaces.
