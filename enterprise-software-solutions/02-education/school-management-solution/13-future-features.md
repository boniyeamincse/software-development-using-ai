# 13 - Roadmap & Future Enhancements

## Phase 1: AI Integration
- Predictive analytics for student performance drop detection.
- Automated timetable optimization using genetic algorithms.

## Phase 2: Advanced Learning
- Integrated LMS for online video courses and interactive SCORM content.
- Gamification of student tasks (badges, leaderboards).

## Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion
- Mobile app for drivers to manage bus routes.
- Biometric integration for automated attendance (Face recognition/Fingerprint).
- Alumni portal and fundraising management.
