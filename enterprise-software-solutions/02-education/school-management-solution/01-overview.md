# 01 - System Overview

## Introduction
The **School Management Solution** is a comprehensive ERP platform designed to streamline administrative, academic, and financial operations for K-12 schools and higher education institutions.

## Problem Statement
Educational institutions often struggle with fragmented data, manual record-keeping, and poor communication between stakeholders (teachers, parents, students, and administration). This leads to inefficiencies and errors in attendance, grading, and fee management.

## Solution
Our system provides a unified digital ecosystem that automates routine tasks, ensures data integrity, and enhances collaboration through real-time notifications and centralized portals.

## Key Objectives
- **Centralize Data**: One source of truth for student and staff records.
- **Automate Workflows**: Digitalize admissions, attendance, and grading.
- **Enhance Communication**: Direct bridge between school and home.
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Reports to monitor academic performance and financial health.
