# 03 - System Modules

## Admin Module
The central hub for managing global settings, staff payroll, inventory, and library systems. Provides high-level analytics dashboards.

## Teacher Module
Tools for attendance marking, lesson planning, grade entry, and direct interaction with assigned classes/students.

## Student Module
A personalized portal where students can view their timetable, download assignments, take online quizzes, and check their results.

## Parent Module
Allows parents to monitor their child's daily attendance, academic progress, upcoming events, and pay school fees online.

## Finance Module
Handles all accounting aspects, including fee collection, staff salary disbursement, and expense tracking.

## Transport & Hostel
Management of school bus routes, student tracking, and dormitory room allocations.
