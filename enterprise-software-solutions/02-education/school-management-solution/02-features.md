# 02 - Core Features

## 1. Student Information System (SIS)
- Complete student profiling including academic history and medical records.
- Digital admission process with document uploads.

## 2. Attendance Management
- Real-time attendance tracking for students and staff.
- Automatic SMS/Email alerts to parents for absences.

## 3. Academic & Examination
- Customizable grading scales (GPA, Percentage, Letter grades).
- Online exam management and digital report card generation.

## 4. Fee & Financial Management
- Online fee payment integration.
- Automated invoice generation and overdue reminders.

## 5. Timetable Management
- Drag-and-drop schedule builder for classes and teachers.
- Conflict detection and substitution management.

## 6. Communication Tools
- Integrated messaging system for teachers and parents.
- Notice board and event calendar.
