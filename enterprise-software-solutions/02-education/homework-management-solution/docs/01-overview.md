# 01 - Homework System Overview

## Introduction
The **Homework Management Solution** is a specialized platform designed to bridge the gap between classroom teaching and independent study. It provides teachers with tools to assign, track, and grade student homework in a digital environment.

## The Problem
Homework management in traditional settings is often chaotic:
- **Misplaced Assignments**: Students lose paper worksheets.
- **Feedback Lag**: Teachers take days to return graded work.
- **Parental Blindness**: Parents are often unaware of upcoming deadlines or their child's completion status.
- **Resource Inequity**: Difficulty in providing varied resources (videos, links) on paper.

## The Solution
A centralized digital hub where assignments are distributed, submitted, and graded. It integrates multimedia resources and provides real-time notifications to all stakeholders.

## Core Objectives
- **Digitalize Submissions**: Eliminating paper waste and lost worksheets.
- **Speed Up Feedback**: Real-time grading and annotation tools for teachers.
- **Increase Transparency**: Real-time dashboards for parents to monitor progress.
- **Resource Enrichment**: Attach videos, PDFs, and interactive links to every assignment.
