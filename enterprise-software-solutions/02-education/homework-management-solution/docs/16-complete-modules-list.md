# 16 - Homework Management Complete Modules List

## 1. Assignment Creation & Distribution
- **Dynamic Task Builder**: Teachers creating rich-media assignments with rubrics.
- **Resource Repository**: Attaching handouts, video links, and templates to tasks.
- **Group Assignment Manager**: Handling collaborative student submissions.

## 2. student Submission Workspace
- **Multi-Format Upload Engine**: Support for docs, code, images, and video.
- **Offline-Sync Client**: Working on homework without a persistent connection.
- **Submission Versioning**: Tracking drafts and final revisions for every task.
- **Mobile OCR Scanner**: converting physical writing into digital submissions.

## 3. Evaluation & Feedback Hub
- **Rubric-Based Grading**: Precise assessment against defined criteria.
- **Annotation & Markup Service**: Direct feedback on documents and code.
- **AI-Enhanced Feedback Assistant**: Generating draft constructive comments.
- **Peer Review Orchestrator**: Anonymized student-to-student evaluation.

## 4. Integrity & Plagiarism Control
- **External Integrity Integration**: Automated scanning for original content.
- **Similarity Reporting Suite**: Visual breakdown of source matching.
- **Metadata Forensics**: Tracking submission timing and device signatures.

## 5. Engagement & Reporting
- **student Progress Dashboard**: Visualizing completion rates and grades.
- **Effort Analytics Engine**: Measuring engagement metrics vs. results.
- **Parent Homework Portal**: Ensuring transparency for families.
- **SIS Grade Bridge**: Syncing homework scores with the primary school record.

## 6. Communication & Alerts
- **Proactive Deadline Manager**: SMS/Email/Push alerts for upcoming tasks.
- **Teacher-Student Chat**: Contextual messaging around specific assignments.
- **Grade Release Control**: Managing when and how students see their scores.
